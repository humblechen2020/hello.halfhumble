Principles · 我的工作原則How I work

這幾件事，是我堅持的。These are the things I hold firm.

  1. 01 Principle

    先拆解、再設計。我會把你以為是「美感」的問題，分類成策略、流程、或溝通的問題；找到根，才有資格動筆。

    Decompose before designing. What you think is an aesthetics problem will be reclassified as strategy, process, or communication. Find the root first.

  2. 02 Principle

    工作是一場對話。我不做「大揭曉式」交付——每階段都會跟你對齊、開放討論。最後成果應該是「我們一起做出來的」，不是被驚喜的。

    Work is a dialogue. No big-reveal deliveries — every phase aligns with you and stays open for discussion. The final result should feel co-created, not surprising.

  3. 03 Principle

    直接、但溫度不變。回饋會直白，但話會留給人。好作品需要安全的開放空間——這是我想跟你一起守住的工作氣氛。

    Direct, but never cold. Feedback is candid, but people come first. Good work needs psychological safety — that's the working climate I want to maintain.

01

Mission.

把品牌的複雜，翻譯成場域裡可被觀看、可被穿越的層次。

Turning the complexity of a brand into layers that can be seen, walked through, and grown into.

02

Values.

清晰、結構、性格、進化——是我的四個底色。

  • Clarity 清晰每個階段有明確交付物，不靠感性「再修一下」推進。Clear deliverables at every stage — not driven by "let's tweak it once more."
  • Structure 結構方法先行：拆解 → 轉譯 → 整合，三段缺一不可。Method first: Decompose → Translate → Integrate — all three steps are non-negotiable.
  • Character 性格不做沒個性的安全牌；每個案件都要有它的「半熟感」。No bland safe choices — every project should carry that distinctly half-humble quality.
  • Evolving 進化所有產出含使用守則，組織內任何人都能繼續長。All deliverables include usage guidelines so anyone in the organisation can keep building from here.
