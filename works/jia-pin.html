嘉品畜牧 — 品牌再造計畫Jiapin Poultry Group — Brand Reinvention

TBrand Identity, Education PAcademic-Industry Project DHanpo Chen Y2026 類別品牌識別 專案性質產學計畫 設計品牌設計:陳翰博 年份2026

Jiapin Animal Husbandry is a third-generation local hatchery in Yunlin with profound expertise in precision incubation, yet it historically lacked a systematic visual identity. As part of the "Small Farmers Alliance 3.0" industry-academia collaboration program, this project aims to establish a comprehensive brand identity for the company. Serving as the Art Director, I managed the team's workflow and collaboratively executed a complete rebranding plan, encompassing brand positioning, the visual identity system, website architecture, and food and agriculture educational tools.

嘉品畜牧為雲林在地傳承三代的老字號孵化場，擁有深厚的精密孵化技術，但過去較缺乏系統化的視覺形象。本專案屬「小農大聯盟 3.0」產學計畫，旨在為其建立完整的品牌識別。我於專案中擔任視覺統籌（Art Director），負責推進團隊進度，共同完成涵蓋品牌定位、識別系統、網站架構以及食農教育教具的品牌再造計畫。

02 — Problem

問題診斷：三十年的技術，沒有一塊招牌能說明它Problem Diagnosis: Thirty Years of Craft, No Sign to Explain It

嘉品的問題不是技術不夠好，而是技術從來沒有被翻譯成外界看得懂的語言——沒有品牌識別、沒有 B2C 銷售窗口，食農推廣也在課程結束的那一刻就斷了線。Jiapin's problem was never the craft — it was that the craft had never been translated into a language outsiders could understand. No brand identity, no B2C sales window, and food-education outreach that broke off the moment each program ended.

第一，嘉品沒有招牌、Logo、視覺系統，外界路過完全不知道這裡在做什麼，這讓 B2B 提案容易失分，B2C 也無從被記憶。第二，唯一面對消費者的管道只有真空塑膠袋裝的蛋品，大量產能始終無法觸及一般消費者，營收結構單一。第三，每一場食農教育課程結束後，孩子帶走的是知識，卻沒有帶走「嘉品」這個名字——教育投入的能量，從來沒有轉化成可被記住的品牌資產。

First, with no signage, logo, or visual system, passers-by had no idea what the operation even was — costing it points in B2B pitches and any chance of being remembered by consumers. Second, the only consumer-facing touchpoint was eggs in plain vacuum-sealed bags, so a large share of capacity never reached everyday buyers, leaving revenue dependent on a single channel. Third, every food-education session ended the same way: kids left with knowledge, but not with the name "Jiapin" — all that educational energy never converted into a lasting brand asset.

03 — Strategy

品牌策略：半開放式場域 × 雙軌品牌語言Brand Strategy — A Semi-Open Facility, Two Brand Voices

半開放式三圈設計：核心封閉 × 邊界可視 × 教育外出Three Concentric Zones: Closed Core × Visible Boundary × Outward Education

場域策略上，我們把嘉品劃成三圈：核心作業區完全封閉，分籠、打預防針等流程以生物安全為最高原則；觀察區可視不可進，用厚玻璃隔離，過道貼近核心，搭配解說牌與視覺化的流程圖；接待／教學區完全開放，從入口、導覽到小教室，完整套用品牌視覺與教具展示。對外的說法很單純：核心封閉是為了保住生物安全，對公眾開放的，是經過安全評估、值得被看見的觀察與教學空間。

We divided the facility into three concentric rings. The core operations zone stays fully closed — sorting, vaccination, and biosecurity come first. The observation zone is visible but not enterable, separated by thick glass with an approach corridor lined with signage and visualized process diagrams. The reception/education zone is fully open, from entrance to guided tour to a small classroom, fully dressed in the new brand visuals and teaching displays. The public framing stays simple: the core stays closed to protect biosecurity; what's open to the public is the observation and teaching space that's been vetted safe and worth seeing.

一個品牌底，兩種對外語言One Brand Foundation, Two Outward Voices

B2B 技術軸主打「穩定與權威」，對象是養殖場、餐商與大型整合業者：強調日本進口設備的精密度、全程監督孵化程序的能力，溝通用語偏專業，訴求穩定供給與三代傳承的公信力。食農教育軸主打「溫暖與傳承」，對象是中小學學童、親子、農會與部落：把深奧的孵化技術轉譯為一場生命教育，用故事感的語氣堆植 B2C 體驗連結，創造長遠的品牌社會資產。兩條語言共用同一套識別系統，只是在不同場合切換音量與用詞。

The B2B technical track speaks "stability and authority" to farms, restaurant buyers, and large integrators — leaning on the precision of imported Japanese equipment and end-to-end incubation oversight, with professional vocabulary built around reliable supply and three generations of credibility. The food-education track speaks "warmth and legacy" to schoolchildren, families, farmers' associations, and indigenous communities — translating dense incubation science into a lesson on life itself, building B2C connection through storytelling that compounds into long-term social brand equity. Both voices share one identity system; only the volume and vocabulary shift by audience.

04 — Design Details

設計深化／識別系統與場域應用Design Details — Identity System & Facility Applications

∞全新主標誌設計New Primary Mark

標誌以蛋形輪廓融合破殼小雞與一雙托住的手，象徵孵化生命與被妥善守護的安心感；中文字標「嘉品畜牧」搭配英文輔助名「Jiapin Poultry Group」，建立可同時用於 B2B 與食農教育場合的單一主商標。

The mark fuses an egg silhouette with a hatching chick cradled in a pair of hands — life emerging, safely held. The Chinese wordmark "嘉品畜牧" pairs with the English endorsement "Jiapin Poultry Group," giving the brand one primary mark fluent in both the B2B and food-education contexts.

∞主色與輔色系統Primary & Secondary Color System

主色調為「恆溫智藍」Pantone 2915 C/U #96CBEE，象徵科技精密、恆溫守護與專業可靠；搭配「破殼雛金」Pantone 1345 C/U #F9C265，象徵生命誕生、溫暖守候與自然蓬勃。輔色延伸出深咖啡與奶油白，並分別設計出白底與深色底的兩套色彩表現版本，確保識別在不同材質與印刷環境下都站得住。

The primary palette pairs "Steady-Warmth Blue" (Pantone 2915 C/U, #96CBEE) — precision, constant-temperature care, professional reliability — with "Hatchling Gold" (Pantone 1345 C/U, #F9C265) — new life, warmth, natural vitality. A secondary palette of deep brown and cream extends the system, with dedicated light-background and dark-background color treatments so the identity holds up across materials and print conditions.

∞標誌變化形式與場域應用Logo Variations & Facility Applications

主標誌延伸出橫式、直式與單色等變化版本，因應招牌、包裝、車身等不同尺度與媒材；產品包裝以金色紋路傳遞品質感，車輛識別則以藍色車身搭配置中標誌，讓品牌在路上與貨架上都維持一致的識讀節奏。

The primary mark extends into horizontal, vertical, and single-color variants for signage, packaging, and vehicle liveries at every scale. Packaging carries a gold-toned pattern that signals quality; vehicle livery uses a blue body with a centered mark, keeping the same reading rhythm whether seen on the road or on a shelf.

∞空間環境與印刷物：制服、場域指標Environment & Print — Uniforms & Facility Signage

員工制服以胸前刺繡徽章搭配深棕／藍色工作服，建立統一的第一印象；場域指標則用深棕底搭配金色標準字，在孵化場、教育中心與展示室之間清楚分流。

Staff uniforms pair an embroidered chest badge with deep-brown or blue workwear for a consistent first impression; facility signage uses a deep-brown ground with a gold wordmark to clearly route visitors between the hatchery, education center, and display room.

∞食農教育周邊：貼紙與拼圖教具Food-Education Collateral — Stickers & Puzzle Kit

「嘉品夢想家」貼紙把雛雞畫成廚師、騎士、偵探等角色，把「孵化」延伸為對成長的期待；拼圖教具結合生命週期與種類辨識的八片式拼圖，讓孩子在遊玩中認識孵化的科學知識。

The "Jiapin Dreamers" sticker set reimagines chicks as chefs, knights, and detectives, extending "hatching" into a hope for growth; the puzzle kit combines life-cycle and species-recognition into an eight-piece set that teaches the science of hatching through play.

05 — Website

網站架構：十大模組的雙軌入口Website — A Ten-Section, Dual-Track Entry Point

網站從首頁主視覺就把訪客分流為 B2B 採購與食農教育兩種對象，再透過品牌雙軌敘事、八大核心優勢、主要孵化品種、限時特賣（直營線）與食農教育生活的第一課（教育線），最後以行動呼籲 Banner 串接聯絡我們，讓兩條語言在同一個品牌底下，各自說服各自要說服的人。

From the homepage hero, visitors are split into two paths — B2B buyers and food-education audiences — then carried through a dual-track brand narrative, eight core strengths, primary hatchery breeds, limited-time offers on the direct-sales track, and "your first lesson in food education" on the education track, before a closing call-to-action banner funnels everyone into contact. Both voices live under one brand, each persuading the audience it's meant to persuade.

06 — Brand Extension

吉祥物延伸與周邊商品Mascot Extension & Merchandise

∞跨主題吉祥物貼圖Cross-Theme Mascot Stickers

把主標誌的雛雞角色延伸成一整套節慶與情境貼圖——耳機、生日帽、聖誕裝、墨鏡——讓品牌在社群與通訊軟體上維持輕鬆、可被分享的存在感。

The primary mark's chick character extends into a full set of seasonal and situational stickers — headphones, birthday hats, Christmas outfits, sunglasses — keeping the brand's presence on social and messaging platforms light and shareable.

∞周邊商品：尺與筆Merchandise — Ruler & Pen

把吉祥物角色做成可實際使用的文具周邊，木尺與公仔筆都延續同一套角色設定，讓識別在桌面日常裡也能被反覆看見。

The mascot characters extend into everyday stationery — a wooden ruler and a figure-topped pen both carry the same cast, keeping the identity visible in daily desktop use.

07 — Conclusion

從場域到桌面，一套識別說兩種話From the Facility to the Desktop, One Identity, Two Voices

從孵化場現場的指標與制服，到名片信封等基本文具，再到社群版型與孩子手中的貼紙，嘉品的品牌再造計畫把雙軌語言落實到每一個會被看見的角落。

From on-site signage and uniforms, to basic stationery like business cards and envelopes, to social templates and the stickers in a child's hand, Jiapin's rebrand carries its dual-track voice into every corner where it's seen.

重點回顧
Key Points

  • 場域策略重新設計：以半開放式三圈場域，在生物安全與公開透明之間找到平衡。
  • Facility strategy redesigned: a semi-open, three-ring layout balances biosecurity with public transparency.
  • 雙軌品牌語言：同一套識別系統，分別對 B2B 與食農教育對象說不同的話。
  • Dual-track brand voice: one identity system speaks differently to B2B buyers and food-education audiences.
  • 網站雙軌入口：從首頁開始分流，讓兩種對象各自走完屬於自己的說服路徑。
  • Dual-track website entry: visitors are split from the homepage on, each path completing its own persuasion journey.
