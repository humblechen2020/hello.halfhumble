Case F02 / 04

TCAPPA — 兒少保護倡議識別TCAPPA — Child Protection Awareness Identity

TBrand, NPO PCase Study DHanpo Chen Y2024 類別品牌識別 專案性質實例專案 設計品牌設計:陳翰博 年份2024

Child abuse prevention is a subject people know they should look at, yet instinctively look away from. Too heavy a visual and readers disengage; too light and the issue gets brushed aside. I pulled the identity language back into a narrow band between warmth and restraint — so the people who need to pay attention don't turn away because of the imagery.

兒虐防治是「應該被看」但常常「不忍直視」的議題。如果視覺太重，讀者會關掉；如果太輕，問題會被輕巧帶過。我把識別語言收斂在溫度與節制之間——讓需要關注的人，不會因為畫面而先轉身。

02 — Problem

專案背景與痛點梳理Background & Problem

「想看」與「想避開」之間只有一線。真正的問題不是議題不被看見，是被看見的方式讓人本能轉身。There's only a thin line between "wanting to look" and "wanting to look away." The real problem isn't that the issue goes unseen — it's that the way it's shown makes people instinctively turn away.

TCAPPA 是長期在第一線處理兒虐通報、家庭重整、社工陪伴的協會。多數人聽過「兒虐」這兩個字，但對組織具體在做什麼、自己能怎麼介入，仍隔著一層霧。原本的識別偏向警告色與圖示化兒童，反而在傳遞訊息前就先把讀者推開。

TCAPPA has long worked the front line of child-abuse reporting, family reintegration, and social-worker support. Most people have heard the words "child abuse," but a fog still hangs over what the organization actually does and how anyone could get involved. The old identity leaned on warning colors and iconified children — which ended up pushing readers away before the message even landed.

協會想做的不是更刺眼的訴求，而是讓「願意停下來看」的人變多。設計要做的，是把那條讀者本能轉身的線，挪到願意停下來的這一邊。

What the association wanted wasn't a louder plea, but more people willing to stop and look. The design's job was to move that instinctive turn-away line further toward the side where people choose to stay.

03 — Strategy

設計策略與語彙系統Design Strategy & Visual Vocabulary

色彩與插畫的節制Restraint in Color & Illustration

主色系從警示紅退一階：以低彩度的暖灰底搭一個飽和但克制的標記色——出現的位置才有警示性，畫面整體保持讀者可進入的溫度。插畫不畫受害者表情，改以「正在發生與正在被陪伴」兩種狀態的構圖語彙：手、距離、靠近，比五官更節制也更普世。

The primary palette steps back one notch from warning red: a low-saturation warm-gray base paired with a single saturated but restrained accent — alarm only appears where it's placed, while the overall image stays approachable. The illustrations skip victims' facial expressions altogether, working instead with the compositional vocabulary of two states — "in the moment" and "being accompanied" — through hands, distance, and closeness, which read as more restrained and more universal than faces.

文字系統與模組化延展Copy System & Modular Extension

文字系統把「我們」改成「你和孩子」，讓讀者意識到自己是場景裡的一個位置，而不只是觀眾。把識別模組化成貼文、海報、影片片頭、文件落款四種尺度，讓社工夥伴在資源有限時也能延展使用。

The copy system swaps "we" for "you and the child," so readers recognize themselves as a position within the scene rather than just an audience. The identity is modularized across four scales — social posts, posters, video title cards, and document sign-offs — so social-work partners can extend it themselves even with limited resources.

04 — Design Details

設計深化／從概念到傳播的實際應用Design Details — From Concept to Applied Communication

∞識別系統設計Identity System Design

建立低彩度暖灰底搭配克制標記色的色彩系統，讓警示性只出現在必要的位置，整體維持讀者可進入的溫度。

We built a color system pairing a low-saturation warm-gray base with a restrained accent, so alarm only surfaces where necessary while the overall feel stays approachable.

∞插畫與應用延伸Illustration & Extended Applications

把「正在發生與正在被陪伴」的構圖語彙落實到海報與社群貼文模板，讓每一次傳播都維持同樣的節制與溫度。

The "in the moment / being accompanied" compositional vocabulary carries through into posters and social post templates, keeping the same restraint and warmth at every touchpoint.

∞視覺規範Visual Guidelines

把識別模組化成貼文、海報、影片片頭、文件落款四種尺度，整理成規範，讓社工夥伴在資源有限的情況下也能自行延展使用。

We modularized the identity into four scales — posts, posters, video title cards, and document sign-offs — and organized them into guidelines, so social-work partners can extend the system themselves even with limited resources.

05 — Conclusion

議題型設計的克制，是讓更多人留下來Restraint in Issue-Driven Design Keeps More People Around

協會回饋：志工招募活動的留下率提升、社群分享率上升，合作媒體也願意採用這套識別作為議題報導的視覺出口。在不靠刺激的前提下被更多人看到——「願意分享」變成這個案子最重要的指標。這個案子讓我確認：議題型設計的克制不是退讓，是讓更多人留下來，當畫面不再用情緒勒索讀者，閱讀者才願意花時間理解結構性問題。

Feedback from the association: volunteer-recruitment retention rose, social shares climbed, and partner media were willing to adopt the identity as the visual face of their own coverage on the issue. Being seen by more people without relying on shock value — "willingness to share" became the most important metric of this project. It confirmed something for me: restraint in issue-driven design isn't backing down, it's what keeps more people around. Once the imagery stops emotionally cornering the reader, they actually have room to spend time understanding the structural problem.

重點回顧
Key Points

  • 色彩與插畫節制：用低彩度暖灰底與克制的標記色，取代警示紅與圖示化受害者表情。
  • Restraint in color & illustration: a low-saturation warm-gray base with a restrained accent replaces warning red and iconified victim expressions.
  • 文字系統重構：把「我們」改成「你和孩子」，讓讀者意識到自己也是場景裡的一個位置。
  • Copy system rebuilt: swapping "we" for "you and the child" so readers recognize themselves as a position within the scene.
  • 模組化延展：四種尺度的識別模組，讓社工夥伴在資源有限時也能自行延展使用。
  • Modular extension: four scales of identity modules let social-work partners extend the system themselves even with limited resources.
