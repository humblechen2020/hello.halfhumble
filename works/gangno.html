港裏 — 復古時尚視覺系統Gangno — Vintage Fashion Visual System

TBrand, Visual System PCase Study DHanpo Chen Y2025 類別品牌識別 專案性質實例專案 設計品牌設計:陳翰博 年份2025

Gangno is a vintage fashion label. The brief was to turn "vintage" from a style keyword into a visual system the brand could reuse again and again — identity, layout, and photography language defined in one pass.

Gangno 是一個復古服飾品牌。這次的任務是把「復古」從風格詞彙，落地成一套品牌可以反覆使用的視覺系統——識別、版型、攝影語言一次定調，讓年代感不只是濾鏡，而是一套可以被執行的設計邏輯。

02 — Problem

專案背景與痛點梳理Background & Problem

「復古」常常被簡化成一層濾鏡或一種懷舊情緒，但對品牌來說，這樣的語言經不起長期、跨物料的使用。"Vintage" often gets reduced to a filter or a nostalgic mood — but for a brand, that kind of language can't hold up to long-term, cross-material use.

「Gangno」這個名字本身就是一組刻意設計的多語言雙關：泰文「แก่」意指「老」；英文拆解成「Gang（群體）」與「no（不）」；韓文同時讀作「江、強大」與「老」——老但強大，所以等於經典。品牌的核心方向定為「逆」：不是為了叛逆而叛逆，而是找回初心，落在極簡的未來與繁瑣的過去之間，定義屬於自己的「現在」。

The name "Gangno" is itself a deliberate multilingual pun: the Thai "แก่" means "old"; in English it splits into "Gang" (a group) and "no" (not); in Korean it reads as both "river / strong" and "old" — old, yet powerful, which adds up to classic. The brand's core direction was defined as "going against the grain" — not rebellion for its own sake, but a way back to first intentions, staking out its own "now" between a minimalist future and an ornate past.

Gangno 在創立初期，復古調性主要仰賴選品與陳列氛圍堆疊出來，沒有一套可以被複製、被延伸的視覺規則。這讓每一次的物料製作——無論是社群貼文、吊牌還是店內陳列——都得重新詮釋一次「復古」的意思，缺乏累積品牌記憶的效率。

In its early days, Gangno's vintage tone relied mainly on product curation and display atmosphere, with no replicable, extensible visual rules. That meant every piece of material — social posts, hang tags, in-store display — had to reinterpret "vintage" from scratch, with no efficient way to build up brand memory.

這次的任務，是把「復古」拆解成可被定義的設計元素：色彩、字體、版面比例、攝影語言，讓它成為一套品牌可以反覆調用的視覺系統，而不是每次都從零開始的氛圍營造。

The task was to break "vintage" down into definable design elements — color, typeface, layout proportions, photography language — turning it into a visual system the brand can call on repeatedly, rather than building the mood from zero every time.

03 — Strategy

設計策略與視覺系統規劃Design Strategy & Visual System Planning

把懷舊情緒轉成設計規則Turning Nostalgia Into Design Rules

從復古年代的印刷品與排版邏輯裡，提煉出可被定義的字級比例與色彩關係，讓「復古感」變成一套有規則可循的視覺語法，而不是憑感覺堆疊的氛圍。

We distilled definable type-scale proportions and color relationships from vintage-era print materials and layout logic, turning "that vintage feeling" into a rule-based visual grammar rather than a mood built by intuition alone.

品牌意象選用老鷹，呼應的是牠逆風而行的飛行智慧——多數鳥類順風滑翔，老鷹卻迎向強風，利用逆風帶來的上升氣流飛得更高。這正是「逆」的具體寫照：把逆境轉成優勢，而非迴避它，也是泰國湖畔老鷹自由、力量與平衡的文化象徵在識別上的延伸。

The eagle was chosen for its instinct to fly into headwinds rather than away from them — most birds glide with the wind, but eagles meet it head-on, riding the updraft it creates to climb higher. That's "going against the grain" made literal: turning resistance into advantage rather than avoiding it, extending the lakeside eagle's cultural symbolism of freedom, power, and balance into the identity itself.

識別、版型、攝影的一致性策略A Consistency Strategy Across Identity, Layout & Photography

識別系統定調之後，同步規劃版型與攝影語言的規則，讓品牌從社群內容到店內陳列，都能用同一套視覺邏輯被辨認出來，降低每次製作物料時重新詮釋風格的成本。

Once the identity system was set, layout and photography rules were planned in parallel, so the brand reads through the same visual logic from social content to in-store display — cutting the cost of reinterpreting the style every time new material is made.

04 — Design Details

設計深化／從概念到系統的實際應用Design Details — From Concept to System Application

∞品牌識別與標準字設計Brand Identity & Wordmark Design

設計呼應復古調性的標準字與輔助圖形，建立一套可延伸至各種物料尺寸的識別系統基礎。

A wordmark and supporting graphics echo the vintage tone, establishing the identity-system foundation that extends across material sizes of every kind.

∞版型與版面系統設計Layout & Grid System Design

建立一套可重複使用的版面網格與比例規則，讓不同尺寸的物料都能在同一套邏輯下被快速產出。

A reusable layout grid and proportion system was established, letting materials of any size be produced quickly under the same logic.
