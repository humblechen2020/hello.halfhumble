就很公平聖誕節Gong Ping Christmas — Interactive Community Poster

TPoster Design, Community PSocial Service DHanpo Chen Y2022 類別海報設計 · 社區參與 專案性質社會服務 設計陳翰博 年份2022

For Gongping Village's Christmas event, the answer to a limited budget and a disconnected community wasn't a better poster — it was a different kind of poster. One that children could color themselves, and whose finished versions would become the event's decorations.

面對宣傳預算有限、世代互動疏離的挑戰，設計策略不是做一張更好的海報，而是做一張不一樣的海報——讓孩子自己填色，讓他們的作品成為活動的佈置，讓創作本身就是最好的宣傳。

02 — Problem

有限預算、疏離的世代、一個聖誕節活動A Limited Budget, a Disconnected Community, One Christmas Event

社區活動的挑戰從來不只是設計，而是怎麼讓人真的走出家門。The challenge of a community event is never just design — it's getting people to actually leave the house.

公平里里長面臨的是典型的地方社區困境：宣傳預算有限，無法走傳統廣告路線；世代之間的互動日漸疏離，老人不知道怎麼跟孩子同樂，孩子不知道鄰居是誰。一場聖誕節活動，如果只靠一張告知訊息的海報，很難讓人覺得「這場活動跟我有關」。

The neighborhood chief faced a classic local-community dilemma: a limited promotional budget ruled out conventional advertising; and a growing generational disconnect meant that elders didn't know how to have fun with children, and children didn't know who their neighbors were. A Christmas event promoted by a single informational poster would struggle to make anyone feel that "this has something to do with me."

公平里的東興公園裡有一隻大橘貓造型的溜滑梯，是這個社區最有辨識度的地標。這隻貓是公平里的吉祥物，也是孩子最熟悉的鄰居。這成為設計的起點。

Dongxing Park in Gongping has a giant orange tabby cat-shaped slide — the neighborhood's most recognizable landmark. The cat is Gongping's mascot, and the neighbor every child knows best. That became the starting point for the design.

03 — Strategy

讓海報本身成為活動Make the Poster the Event

把海報設計成填色稿Design the Poster as a Color-in Template

海報的設計策略不是「做一張更好看的海報」，而是「讓居民自己完成這張海報」。設計師提供的是骨架——吉祥物大橘貓、聖誕樹、活動資訊——孩子拿到的是一張等待被填滿的白描稿，可以用自己喜歡的顏色自由繪製。

The strategy wasn't "make a better-looking poster" — it was "let residents complete the poster themselves." The designer provides the skeleton: the cat mascot, the Christmas tree, the event information. Children receive a blank line-art version, free to color however they like.

參與感就是最好的宣傳Participation Is the Best Promotion

當孩子親手完成一張海報，活動就不再只是一個「里長舉辦的活動」，而是「我也有參與的活動」。家長帶著孩子一起繪製，本身就是活動前的熱身互動；完成的作品掛在活動現場，孩子自然會拉著家人去找自己畫的那一張——宣傳與佈置，一次完成。

When a child completes a poster by hand, the event is no longer just "something the neighborhood chief organized" — it's "something I was part of." Parents and children coloring together become a warm-up interaction before the event begins; completed works hung at the venue naturally pull kids (and their families) back to find their own piece. Promotion and decoration: done in one move.

04 — 海報設計

就很公平聖誕節 — 主視覺Gong Ping Christmas — Key Visual

海報的主角是那隻大家都認識的橘貓，戴上聖誕帽，坐在聖誕樹旁邊，等著你來填滿它的顏色。The poster's star is the orange cat everyone knows — Santa hat on, sitting by the Christmas tree, waiting for you to fill in its colors.

配色選用聖誕節的紅綠撞色作為底調，加上萊姆黃作為活動資訊色塊，製造出強烈的視覺辨識度。標題「就很公平聖誕節」以手繪感字型直排，讓整個版面帶有一種隨性的社區溫度，而不是制式的官方公告。左側對話泡泡裡的小文案——「我想成為你心中的聖誕樹」、「天氣涼涼的，聖誕裡上穿你的衣服嗎」——讓橘貓有了自己的個性。

The palette uses the classic red-green Christmas contrast as a base, with lime yellow for the event information block — creating strong visual recognition. The title 就很公平聖誕節 runs vertically in a hand-drawn typeface, giving the layout the relaxed warmth of a community notice rather than an official announcement. Speech bubbles on the left — "I want to be the Christmas tree in your heart," "It's getting cool — are you wearing a jacket to the Christmas neighborhood?" — give the cat its own personality.

05 — 兒童創作

孩子畫的那幾十張，就是這次的展覽Those Dozens of Children's Drawings Were the Exhibition

橘貓可以是橘色的，也可以是藍色的、彩虹色的、黑色的。每個孩子的版本都不一樣，每一張都對。The cat could be orange, blue, rainbow-colored, or black. Every child's version was different, and every version was right.

孩子們拿到填色版海報後，用彩色鉛筆、蠟筆、色筆自由繪製。有人讓橘貓穿上了聖誕紅，有人把聖誕樹畫成彩色條紋，有人把整張背景塗成黑色讓圖案更突出。完成的作品統一用夾子吊掛在活動現場，一排排的，成為整個聖誕節活動最真實的主視覺。

Children received the color-in poster and filled it with colored pencils, crayons, and markers. Some dressed the cat in Christmas red; some made the Christmas tree rainbow-striped; some filled the whole background black to make the figures pop. Finished pieces were clipped and hung in rows at the venue — becoming the most honest key visual the Christmas event could have.

06 — 活動現場

人來了They Came

2021 年 12 月 19 日，東興公園人潮滿滿。聖誕老人在中央帶動唱，孩子們圍在旁邊一邊鼓掌一邊找自己的那一張海報，家長拿著手機拍下孩子的作品和笑臉。那幾十張掛在上方的填色海報，成了整個活動最自然的背景，也是最好的紀錄。

On December 19, 2021, Dongxing Park was packed. Santa led the crowd in songs at center; children clapped along while hunting for their own poster; parents lifted their phones to capture both the artwork and the smiles. Those dozens of hand-colored posters hanging overhead became the most natural backdrop the event could have asked for — and its best record.

重點回顧
Key Points

  • 參與即宣傳：把海報設計成兒童填色稿，讓創作過程本身就成為活動宣傳的一部分，解決低預算的傳播困境。
  • Participation as promotion: designing the poster as a children's color-in template makes the creation process itself part of the event's promotion, solving the low-budget reach problem.
  • 吉祥物作為設計核心：以東興公園的橘貓溜滑梯作為海報主角，讓社區居民產生立即的歸屬感與辨識度。
  • Mascot as design anchor: centering the poster on the park's orange cat slide gives residents immediate recognition and a sense of belonging.
  • 一稿兩用：孩童完成的填色作品同時作為活動佈置，讓設計產出最大化——宣傳物料、展覽作品、互動記憶，三者合一。
  • One deliverable, two uses: children's finished pieces double as event décor — promotional material, exhibition, and participatory memory, all in one.
