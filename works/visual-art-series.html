Case L02 / 15

根光Rooted Light

TVideo Art, Installation PLab · Self-initiated DHanpo Chen Y2024 類別影像裝置藝術 專案性質自主創作 設計陳翰博 年份2024

A visual journey through growth, tension, and release. Rooted Light unfolds across four emotional chapters — Awakening, Growth, Wavering, Embrace — using post-impressionist color philosophy, immersive dual-screen projection, and the tension between painted image and photographic reality to trace one consciousness across an entire life.

以梵谷後印象派的色彩哲學為骨架，透過沉浸式雙螢幕投影，交織寫實影像與心靈系大地景觀，探索人的一生意識——萌醒、生長、徬徨、接納。影像如筆觸堆疊，回應生命光影與情感的輪迴流動。

02 — Full Film

觀看完整作品Watch the Full Film

從新生到落幕，這是一場由內而外的情感遷徙。From rebirth to farewell, this is an inward migration of emotion.

影像、聲音與意象，層層交疊，讓身體成為光與根之間的容器。本作於學學文創一樓拉門以雙螢幕沉浸式投影方式呈現，左右各寬350cm、高318cm，大多時候同時投影不一樣的方形畫面，少數畫面會一起投成長方形畫面。另有半透明的畫面C不定時飄移於AB之中。

Visuals, sounds, and metaphors intertwine — the body becomes a vessel between light and root. The work was presented as an immersive dual-screen projection at Xuexue Institute's ground-floor sliding doors, each screen 350cm wide × 318cm tall. Most of the time the two screens show different square compositions; occasionally they merge into a single widescreen image, while a semi-transparent overlay drifts unpredictably between them.

03 — Concept

梵谷色彩哲學 × 人生意識的四個章節Van Gogh's Color Philosophy × Four Chapters of Consciousness

靈感取自梵谷的〈向日葵〉——大量高彩度的黃色與橘色，標誌性的短促、快速且充滿力量的筆觸。在《根光》中，我們借鑑梵谷的色彩哲學，用這種充滿能量的筆觸描繪「根」的生長與「光」的爆發，以表達情感階段的變化。

Inspired by Van Gogh's Sunflowers — high-chroma yellows and oranges, signature rapid and forceful brushstrokes. In Rooted Light, we borrow Van Gogh's color philosophy: those energy-charged marks trace the growth of roots and the eruption of light, carrying each emotional stage of transformation.

一二章後印象派筆觸，三四章走向光與攝影的實體Chapters 1–2: Post-impressionist paint. Chapters 3–4: Toward light and photographic reality.

影像如筆觸堆疊——一二章後梵谷印象派的繪畫元素較多，呈現土的元素（成長階段）；到後面越來越接近光，畫面也就越實體，更多的攝影素材（photo(光)graphic(描寫)）。寫實與幻象交錯，然後慢慢形成具體。

Images stack like brushstrokes. Chapters 1–2 lean heavily on post-impressionist painting — earth, soil, the growing stage. Toward the end, the frame moves closer to light, growing more physical, with more photographic material. Reality and illusion interweave, then slowly solidify.

04 — Four Chapters

章節拆解 · 從土壤到光Chapter by Chapter · From Earth to Light

Chapter 01 · 成長階段 · 暖黃 赭石 土褐

萌醒 Awakening

沉睡於柔軟的土壤，裹著微光的薄衣。
無數初生的靈魂，輕輕被喚醒，迎向晨曦——
卻也觸碰真相的刺。
Resting in the tender embrace of earth, cloaked in a delicate sheen of light.
Countless nascent souls are softly roused, greeting the dawn—
yet brushing against the thorns of truth.

從現實殘像展開：急促上班的腳步、社畜日常、魚池中無目的游動，象徵被困的精神狀態。隨著影像推進，畫面採後印象派筆觸，由泥土出發，逐步向光靠近。最後帶入尼采精神三變——駱駝的承受、獅子的對抗，最終化為嬰兒的重生。

Opening from the residual images of reality: the hurried steps of a commute, the daily grind, fish circling aimlessly in a pond — symbols of a trapped mental state. As the images advance, the frame adopts post-impressionist brushwork, starting from earth and gradually moving toward light. It concludes with Nietzsche's Three Metamorphoses — the endurance of the camel, the resistance of the lion, finally the rebirth of the infant.

Chapter 02 · 張力階段 · 梵谷色彩 綠 藍 黃

生長 Growth

追逐天際的輝芒，纖細的根鬚，
蜿蜒、舒展，只為一瞬陽光的擁抱。
在每一道裂縫中，織出屬於自己的溫柔光景。
Chasing the glow of the horizon, slender roots twist and reach,
yearning for a fleeting embrace of sunlight.
In every crevice, they weave a gentle scene of their own.

開頭AB畫面同時呈現大橫幅的梵谷畫作風格樹根及大自然，後分割畫面為眼睛再進入漩渦圖案。瞳孔裡的表情符號指出情緒的變化與轉折——這是如青少年般的階段，渴望向光卻尚未穩定，越是努力生長，根便向下蔓延得更深。

Opening with both AB screens presenting a large panoramic image of Van Gogh-style tree roots and nature, then splitting into eyes and spiraling patterns. Emoji in the pupils signal emotional shifts — this is the adolescent stage: yearning for light but not yet stable; the harder the growth, the deeper the roots reach down.

Chapter 03 · 張力階段 · 枯黃 暗藍 陰影灰

徬徨 Wavering

日復一日，於遺憾中踱步。
風聲在耳畔低吟，無數目光凝視一場絢爛的舞臺，
期待你的光芒萬丈，也靜待你的黯然隕落。
Day after day, pacing through regret.
The wind hums softly at the ear, countless gazes fixed on a radiant stage,
awaiting your brilliance, and silently watching for your fall.

帶入更多具像畫面，引用許多電影或藝術的具象素材，展現情感的困擾。從微生物與瞳孔般的抽象，擴展為更具象的意象，象徵意識逐漸成形。伴隨而來的是情感的困擾與對他人眼光的敏感——有些是真實的注視，有些則是內心的投射與想像。

More figurative images enter — film references, art citations — depicting emotional turmoil. Abstract microbe-and-pupil forms expand into concrete imagery, symbolizing a consciousness solidifying. Alongside: emotional distress and sensitivity to others' gazes — some real, some projected from within.

↑ 三-四章轉場：AB雙螢幕同步畫面，引導情感從徬徨進入接納的過渡。

↑ Ch3→4 Transition: A and B screens in sync, guiding the emotional shift from Wavering into Embrace.

Chapter 04 · 掙扎釋放 · 金色 亮白 淺青

接納 Embrace

枯槁的雙手，輕撫新生生命的脈動。
最終篇章，以無聲的輝煌落幕。
Withered hands cradle the pulse of new life.
The final verse.

象徵生命歷程的圓滿與重生。此階段主角已成熟，擁有孕育新種子的能力，外在堅定而溫柔，內心卻回到如嬰兒般最純淨、最安心的狀態。影像風格以溫暖的光波、緩慢的手勢與碎片化的記憶重組構成，營造出包覆、撫慰與釋懷的氛圍。

Symbolizing the fullness and rebirth of a life's journey. The protagonist is now mature, capable of nurturing new seeds — outwardly steadfast and gentle, inwardly returned to the purest, most secure infant state. The visual style is built from warm light waves, slow gestures, and fragmented memories reassembling.

05 — Exhibition · 學學文創一樓拉門

雙螢幕沉浸式投影 · 350cm × 318cm × 2Dual-Screen Immersive Projection · 350cm × 318cm × 2

展出場域為學學文創一樓拉門空間，左右各一面牆作為獨立投影面。觀者可自由移動於兩面屏幕之間，成為影像的一部分——其輪廓被光影吞沒，身體即是容器，光與根在此流動穿越。

The exhibition space was the sliding-door wall at Xuexue Institute's ground floor. Viewers moved freely between the two projection surfaces — silhouettes absorbed by light and shadow, the body becoming a vessel through which root and light pass.

06 — Conclusion

從新生到落幕，這是一場由內而外的情感遷徙From Rebirth to Farewell, an Inward Migration of Emotion

影像、聲音與意象，層層交疊，讓身體成為光與根之間的容器。《根光》不是一個關於答案的作品，而是一個關於行走的作品——沿著情感的路徑，在每一個章節停留，然後繼續前進。

Visuals, sounds, and metaphors intertwine — the body becomes a vessel between light and root. Rooted Light is not a work about answers. It is a work about walking — staying in each emotional chapter, then moving on.

重點回顧
Key Points

  • 後印象派色彩系統：借鑑梵谷的對比色哲學，以顏色溫度標記每個情感階段的轉折，暖黃 → 枯黃暗藍 → 金色亮白。
  • Post-impressionist color system: Van Gogh's contrasting color philosophy marks each emotional stage's shift — warm yellow → withered yellow / dark blue → gold and bright white.
  • 繪畫到攝影的連續體：前兩章以筆觸質感呈現泥土與根，後兩章轉向光的寫實攝影，象徵意識從夢境走向現實。
  • Painting-to-photography continuum: the first two chapters present earth and root through brushwork texture; the last two shift toward photographic reality of light — consciousness moving from dream to the real.
  • 雙螢幕沉浸投影：AB兩屏各自獨立，時而合而為一的大橫幅；另有半透明層C飄移其中，三個維度的畫面構建出一個立體的情感場域。
  • Dual-screen immersive projection: screens A and B operate independently, occasionally merging into a single panorama; a semi-transparent layer C drifts between them — three visual dimensions building a three-dimensional emotional field.
