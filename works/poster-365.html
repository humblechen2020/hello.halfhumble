365 海報設計365 Days of Posters

TLab, Editorial PSelf-Initiated DHanpo Chen Y2024 類別海報設計 專案性質自主專案 設計視覺設計:陳翰博 年份2024

365 Days of Posters is a year-long personal visual journal — one poster a day, turning raw experimentation into disciplined practice, and disciplined practice into a recognizable style.

一年 365 天連續產出的個人視覺日誌——把實驗變成練習，把練習變成風格。這是一個以海報為媒介的自主設計練習，每天用一張畫面測試一個構圖、字體或色彩的想法，累積成一套個人視覺語彙的養成紀錄。

02 — Problem

專案背景與痛點梳理Background & Problem

設計練習常常因為缺乏固定節奏而中斷，靈感與技法的累積也很難被系統化地檢視。Design practice often stalls without a fixed rhythm, and the buildup of inspiration and technique is hard to review systematically.

大多數的個人創作練習，會在沒有外部壓力的情況下逐漸停滯。靈感來得不規律，技法的成長也缺乏一個可以回頭檢視的軌跡。如何建立一套足夠輕量、卻能持續一整年的產出機制，讓每一天的練習都能被記錄、被比較，是這個專案最初要解決的問題。

Most personal creative practices gradually stall without external pressure. Inspiration arrives unpredictably, and there's no trail to review how technique is developing. The first problem this project had to solve was building a mechanism light enough to sustain for a full year, yet capable of recording and comparing every day's practice.

任務是設計一套每日海報的產出規則與視覺框架，讓 365 天的練習可以累積成一個連貫的作品系列，而不是 365 張各自獨立的圖。

The task was to design a set of rules and a visual framework for daily posters, so 365 days of practice would accumulate into one coherent series rather than 365 unrelated images.

03 — Strategy

設計策略與系列規劃Design Strategy & Series Planning

每日練習的框架設計Framework Design for Daily Practice

建立一套固定的尺寸與基礎網格，讓每天的創作可以在限制之內自由發揮，同時確保 365 張海報排在一起時仍有視覺上的連貫性。

We set a fixed size and base grid, giving each day's creation room to improvise within a constraint, while keeping all 365 posters visually coherent when displayed together.

主題輪替與產出策略Theme Rotation & Output Strategy

以週為單位輪替不同的設計母題——字體實驗、色彩練習、構圖挑戰——讓長時間的產出維持新鮮感，也讓整年的系列呈現出階段性的風格演變。

Design motifs rotate on a weekly basis — typography experiments, color exercises, composition challenges — keeping long-term output fresh and letting the year-long series show a staged evolution of style.

04 — Design Details

設計深化／從單張練習到系列風格Design Details — From Single Exercises to a Series Style

∞動態效果與系列轉場Motion Sequences & Series Transitions

把連續多天的海報剪成動態展示，用翻頁般的節奏呈現風格逐日演變的過程，靜態畫面排在一起時不容易察覺的細微調整，在動態裡反而看得更清楚。

Consecutive days of posters were cut into motion sequences, paging through like a flipbook to reveal how the style evolved day by day — subtle shifts easy to miss in static grids become much clearer in motion.

∞色彩語彙的累積Building a Color Vocabulary

透過大量重複的色彩配對練習，逐漸找出屬於個人的色彩偏好與使用邏輯，並記錄下哪些組合在不同主題下最具表現力。

Through extensive, repeated color-pairing exercises, a personal color preference and logic gradually emerged, with notes on which combinations were most expressive under different themes.

∞系列彙整與風格梳理Series Compilation & Style Refinement

每隔一段時間回頭整理已完成的海報，挑出反覆出現的視覺習慣，將其有意識地強化成個人風格的核心元素。

Periodically reviewing completed posters surfaced recurring visual habits, which were then consciously reinforced into core elements of a personal style.

05 — Conclusion

把每日練習累積成個人風格的證據Turning Daily Practice Into Evidence of a Personal Style

365 天的海報練習，最終累積成的不只是數量，而是一套可以被辨認出來的個人視覺語彙。從最初不確定的嘗試，到後期愈來愈清楚的選擇，這個系列記錄了風格如何在持續的重複與調整中逐漸成形。

365 days of poster practice ultimately built up not just quantity, but a recognizable personal visual vocabulary. From uncertain early attempts to increasingly deliberate choices later on, this series records how a style gradually forms through sustained repetition and adjustment.

重點回顧
Key Points

  • 每日產出機制：建立固定尺寸與基礎網格，讓一年的練習維持視覺連貫性。
  • Daily output mechanism: a fixed size and base grid keep a year of practice visually coherent.
  • 主題輪替策略：以週為單位輪替設計母題，兼顧新鮮感與系列的階段性演變。
  • Theme rotation strategy: weekly rotating design motifs balance freshness with the series' staged evolution.
  • 個人風格梳理：定期回顧並強化反覆出現的視覺習慣，逐步沉澱出個人語彙。
  • Personal style refinement: regular review and reinforcement of recurring visual habits gradually distilled a personal vocabulary.
