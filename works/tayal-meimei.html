Case F03 / 04

泰雅妹妹 — 舞團形象視覺設計Tayal Meimei — Dance Troupe Visual Identity

TVisual Identity, Cultural PCase Study DHanpo Chen Y2025 類別形象識別設計 專案性質實例專案 設計品牌設計:陳翰博 年份2025

Tayal Meimei is a dance troupe carrying Tayal weaving culture onto the stage. This project builds the troupe's visual identity — translating the logic of traditional patterns into a mark, a color system, and a photographic language that can represent the troupe across posters, programs, and performance documentation.

把泰雅族的織紋文化，轉化成一個舞團真正能拿出去使用的形象識別。任務是替泰雅妹妹舞團，建立一套從標誌、色彩到攝影語言都站得住的視覺系統，讓傳統圖紋背後的文化份量，能夠代表這個正在舞台上持續演出的當代團體。

02 — Problem

專案背景與痛點梳理Background & Problem

舞團的識別常常被簡化成「貼上族群圖紋」，卻沒有真正回答「這個舞團是誰」的問題。Dance troupe identities often get reduced to "slap on some ethnic patterns," without ever really answering the question of who this troupe actually is.

泰雅妹妹舞團的核心是文化傳承與當代表演的結合，但市場上多數原住民主題的視覺，容易停留在圖紋裝飾的層次，沒有進一步發展成一套真正屬於舞團本身、可以重複使用的識別系統。舞團需要的不是一張漂亮的海報，是一套能跟著團隊走過每一場演出的視覺語言。

Tayal Meimei's core is the fusion of cultural heritage and contemporary performance, but most indigenous-themed visuals in the market stay at the level of pattern decoration, never developing into a reusable identity system that truly belongs to the troupe itself. What the troupe needed wasn't one good-looking poster — it was a visual language that could travel with them through every performance.

這次的任務，是先確認舞團要被記住的是什麼——是圖紋本身，還是圖紋背後那群正在舞台上演出的人——再決定識別系統該往哪個方向發展。

The task was first to pin down what the troupe should be remembered for — the patterns themselves, or the people performing on stage behind them — and only then decide which direction the identity system should grow in.

03 — Strategy

設計策略與識別系統規劃Design Strategy & Identity System Planning

圖紋系統化為識別語言Turning Pattern Into Identity Language

把傳統織紋拆解成可重複組合的基本單元，發展成標誌與圖形系統的基礎語彙，讓圖紋不再只是裝飾，而是舞團識別本身的骨架。

We broke traditional weaving patterns down into reusable, recombinable base units, developing them into the foundational vocabulary of the logo and graphic system — so the patterns are no longer mere decoration, but the very skeleton of the troupe's identity.

表演視覺與當代轉譯Performance Visuals & Contemporary Translation

把識別系統延伸到舞台攝影與宣傳視覺的構圖邏輯裡，透過版面節奏與留白，讓族群文化的份量感在當代演出語境裡依然清晰可辨，不被簡化成單純的裝飾元素。

We extended the identity system into the compositional logic of stage photography and promotional visuals. Through layout rhythm and negative space, the cultural weight of the patterns stays clearly legible within a contemporary performance context, instead of being reduced to a purely decorative element.

04 — Design Details

設計深化／兩套形象風格：單寧 × 紳士Design Details — Two Visual Personas: Denim × Gentleman

∞雙風格定位A Two-Persona Direction

這次為舞團發展出兩套並行的形象風格——單寧風與紳士風。兩者都走現代化的視覺語言，卻在時尚的外殼底下，藏著原住民獨有的青春與野性，讓同一群人可以在不同場合切換出不同的氣場。

We developed two parallel visual personas for the troupe — a denim look and a gentleman look. Both speak a modern visual language, yet beneath the stylish surface each carries the youthful, untamed energy unique to indigenous identity, letting the same group switch between very different moods depending on the occasion.

∞單寧風／時尚．活力．青春再現Denim — Stylish, Energetic, Youth Revived

單寧風主打街頭感與爆發力，藍色丹寧的層疊穿搭搭配明亮俐落的背景，把舞團「時尚、活力、青春再現」的一面拉到最滿，是最貼近觀眾印象中舞者能量的版本。

The denim look leans into street energy and raw drive — layered blue denim against a bright, crisp backdrop pushes the troupe's "stylish, energetic, youth revived" side to its peak, the version closest to the dancer energy audiences picture.

∞紳士風／成熟．俐落．野性收進剪裁Gentleman — Mature, Sharp, Wildness in the Cut

紳士風以黑色西裝與低彩度的黑白影像，把同一群人帶到成熟、俐落的另一端。鋒利的剪裁壓著張力，野性沒有消失，而是收進線條裡，展現舞團不只有青春、也撐得起場面的一面。

The gentleman look uses black tailoring and low-saturation black-and-white imagery to carry the same group to a mature, sharp opposite end. Crisp cuts hold the tension in — the wildness doesn't disappear, it's folded into the lines, showing a troupe that has more than youth and can command a room.

05 — Conclusion

讓族群文化的份量，站上舞台也站得住Letting Cultural Weight Hold Up on Stage

這個自主專案，把泰雅妹妹舞團從「視覺上的族群符號」，轉變成一個有完整識別系統、可被持續使用的當代表演團體。圖紋系統化的過程，也讓這次設計重新理解文化轉譯不只是視覺上的相似，更是結構與份量的保留。

This self-initiated project transformed Tayal Meimei from a "visual ethnic symbol" into a contemporary performance troupe with a complete, sustainably usable identity system. The process of systemizing the patterns also reshaped how this design understood cultural translation — not just visual resemblance, but the preservation of structure and weight.

重點回顧
Key Points

  • 圖紋系統化：把傳統織紋拆解成可重複組合的模組，作為識別系統的基礎語彙。
  • Pattern systemization: traditional weaving patterns broken into recombinable modules as the base vocabulary of the identity system.
  • 舞團識別建立：發展出標誌、色彩與圖形系統，讓舞團對外露出時有一致的視覺語氣。
  • Troupe identity built: a logo, color, and graphic system developed so the troupe presents a consistent visual voice publicly.
  • 表演影像延伸：統一攝影與宣傳物料的構圖邏輯，讓識別延伸到舞台與宣傳的每個接觸點。
  • Performance imagery extension: unified composition logic for photography and promotional materials, extending the identity to every touchpoint on stage and in promotion.
