良全 — 年度茶葉禮盒包裝設計Liang Quan — Annual Tea Gift Box Packaging

TPackaging, Brand PCase Study DHanpo Chen PhZhou Yanzhang / Liu Huanjun / Chen HanPo Y2025 類別包裝設計 專案性質實例專案 設計包裝設計：陳翰博 攝影周彥彰 / 劉奐均 / 陳翰博 年份2025

After Liang Quan's identity system landed, the company wanted a year-end ritual — an annual gift box for clients and partners that carries the brand's tone instead of generic corporate gifting. This edition centers on tea, built on the brand's own calligraphy: four tea packages, four characters, four values.

良全的識別系統落地之後，業主希望建立每年固定的送禮儀式——在年末給客戶與夥伴一份真正帶著品牌調性的禮盒。這一版本以茶葉為主題，以品牌書法字為核心語言，四款小包裝對應「良全預拌」四字，每字各自承載一句品牌精神。

02 — Problem

如何讓混凝土廠說出一份有溫度的禮How a Concrete Company Delivers a Gift with Warmth

良全的識別建立在工程語境裡——線條明確、調性穩重。年度禮盒要做的，是讓同一套識別語彙跨進送禮場景，而不是另起爐灶。Liang Quan's identity was built for an engineering context — clean lines, a steady tone. The gift box needed to carry that same vocabulary into a gifting scenario, not replace it.

收禮的對象並不是業主日常往來的工程夥伴，而是那些業主想表達誠意的關係人：長期客戶、重要合作方、年末想特別致謝的對象。包裝在第一眼就得傳遞出用心，讓人感覺到背後有人花時間想過這份禮的每一層設計。

The recipients aren't the client's day-to-day engineering partners — they're the people the client wants to show genuine appreciation to: long-term clients, key collaborators, relationships worth thanking at year's end. The packaging has to convey care at first glance, giving the sense that someone thought through every layer of this gift.

設計最大的挑戰不是把禮盒做得好看，而是在一份茶葉禮裡讓人自然感受到：這是良全送的，而且只有良全才能送出這樣的一份禮。

The real challenge wasn't making a beautiful gift box — it was making a tea gift where recipients instinctively sense: this is from Liang Quan, and only Liang Quan could give a gift that feels like this.

03 — Strategy

堅實中蘊藏溫度Solid Warmth Within

以書法字作為語彙橋樑Calligraphy as a Vocabulary Bridge

良全的品牌商標本身就帶有書法字的痕跡，這成為連結工業感與人文感的關鍵線索。設計以書法筆觸為主視覺語彙，放大後作為小包裝的主體圖案，讓混凝土廠的品牌字跡直接轉化為禮盒上的視覺質感。

Liang Quan's brand mark already carries the trace of calligraphy — this became the key thread connecting industrial identity with human warmth. The design amplifies calligraphic brushwork as the primary visual vocabulary, scaling it up to fill each small package, letting the concrete company's own brand script transform directly into the texture of the gift.

四字一禮：「良全預拌」化為四款包裝Four Characters, One Gift Set

品牌名稱「良全預拌」四字，恰好對應四款茶葉小包裝，每字各自承載一句品牌精神：良——良心品質，值得信賴；全——全力以赴，精準到位；拌——拌築堅實，締造非凡；預——預籌未來，穩固根基。四款疊進同一個外盒，拆開的瞬間才發現這份禮盒在說整個品牌的故事。

The four characters of the brand name "Liang Quan Yu Ban" map exactly to four individual tea packages — each carrying one brand philosophy: 良 (Liang) — Quality of Conscience, Worth Trusting; 全 (Quan) — All-out Effort, Precise Execution; 拌 (Ban) — Building Solid, Creating Extraordinary; 預 (Yu) — Planning for the Future, Solidifying the Foundation. All four nest inside a single outer box — open it, and you realize this gift is telling the entire brand's story.

可延伸的年度禮盒結構An Annual System Built to Last

外盒尺寸、開箱動作與內部卡槽邏輯先固定成不限定內容物的骨架，保留識別系統的幾何邏輯與配色關係，往後每年只需更換主題圖案與內容物，不必重新設計結構。今年是茶，明年可以是任何東西——但每年打開的感覺都是良全的感覺。

Box dimensions, the unboxing sequence, and liner slot logic were fixed first into a content-agnostic skeleton that preserves the identity system's geometric logic and color relationships. Each following year only requires swapping the theme and contents, never the structure. This year it's tea — next year it could be anything — but every year the feeling of opening it is unmistakably Liang Quan.

04 — Design Details

設計深化／從材質到細節的每一層決策Design Details — Every Decision from Material to Detail

∞外盒：灰牛皮紙板 × 白色燙白 × 紅色腰封Outer Box: Gray Kraft × White Foil Stamp × Red Belly Band

外盒選用灰色牛皮紙板，呼應混凝土的質地，保留工業感的同時回歸自然材質。外盒正面採白色燙白加打凹工藝，讓品牌商標在光線下隱約浮現；深紅色腰封以放大的書法字為底紋，套上外盒後在視覺上形成強烈的新舊對話。

The outer box uses gray kraft board — resonating with concrete's texture while returning to a natural material. The top face features white hot-foil stamping with debossing, letting the brand mark emerge subtly in light. A deep-red belly band printed with large calligraphy characters as texture slips over the box, creating a sharp visual conversation between old and new.

∞四款小包裝：良、全、預、拌Four Individual Packages: 良、全、拌、預

每款小包裝以放大的品牌書法字作為頂面主視覺，正面側邊印上對應的品牌精神短句，背面印製泡茶方法。灰色環保紙板、白色燙白商標，與書法字的手感筆觸形成精緻而不失溫度的包裝語言。

Each small package features the brand's enlarged calligraphic character as its top surface, the corresponding brand philosophy printed on the front side, and brewing instructions on the back. Gray eco-board, white foil-stamped logo, and the handmade quality of calligraphic brushwork combine into a packaging language that is refined without losing warmth.

∞細節工藝：打凹、燙白、手寫卡Craft Details: Deboss, Foil, Handwritten Card

側邊打凹「100%善生環境友善紙板」，在不印刷的區域傳遞出對用料的誠意；小包裝頂部以白色燙白商標取代印刷，在灰色紙面上形成低調的品牌識別。禮盒內附業主親筆手寫卡，以混凝土廠房照片為背景，寫下致謝與心意——設計收尾的那一筆，交給了人情本身。

The side edge is debossed with "100% eco-friendly board" — communicating material integrity without printing. The small packages bear white foil-stamped logos on top rather than printed ink, creating a low-key brand mark on the gray surface. Inside the box, a handwritten card from the company's owner uses the concrete factory as its backdrop, with a personal message of thanks — the final touch of the design is handed back to human gesture.

05 — Conclusion

一份能每年重新說故事的禮A Gift That Retells Its Story Every Year

這份茶葉禮盒把良全原本服務於工程語境的識別語彙，透過材質選擇與書法字的放大應用，轉譯成一份有溫度、有故事、有品牌識別度的年度送禮提案。更重要的是，設計留下的是一套可延伸的結構——往後每年更換主題，良全仍然有辦法說出同一句話：這份禮，是我們才能送出去的禮。

This tea gift box translates Liang Quan's engineering-rooted identity vocabulary — through material choices and the amplification of calligraphic brushwork — into an annual gifting proposal that carries warmth, story, and unmistakable brand recognition. More importantly, what it leaves behind is an extensible structure: each year with a new theme, Liang Quan can still say the same thing — this gift is one only we could give.

重點回顧
Key Points

  • 書法字為橋樑：放大品牌商標的書法筆跡，成為連結工業感與人文溫度的核心視覺語彙。
  • Calligraphy as bridge: amplifying the brand mark's calligraphic brushwork became the core visual vocabulary connecting industrial identity with human warmth.
  • 四字一禮：「良全預拌」四字化為四款小包裝，每字承載一句品牌精神，整組禮盒說完整個品牌的故事。
  • Four characters, one gift: the four characters of "Liang Quan Yu Ban" become four individual packages, each carrying a brand philosophy — together the gift set tells the whole brand story.
  • 工藝細節的誠意：燙白、打凹、環保紙板，每一層材料選擇都在說明這份禮背後有人認真想過。
  • Craft as sincerity: foil stamping, debossing, eco-board — every material choice signals that someone thought seriously about this gift.
  • 可延伸的年度系統：結構固定，主題年年可換，讓良全從此有辦法每年都送出一份真正屬於品牌的禮。
  • An annual system built to extend: the structure is fixed, the theme changes every year — so Liang Quan can give a genuinely on-brand gift for every year to come.
