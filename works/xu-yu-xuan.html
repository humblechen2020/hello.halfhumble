許育璿 — 地方競選品牌形象識別設計Xu Yu-Xuan — Local Campaign Brand Identity Design

TBrand, Campaign PCase Study DHanpo Chen Y2024 類別品牌識別 專案性質實例專案 設計品牌設計:陳翰博 年份2024

This project builds a full campaign brand identity for local candidate Xu Yu-Xuan — moving the visual language away from generic "passionate" campaign cues and toward something that reads as steady and trustworthy. The goal wasn't louder — it was more consistent, so the same face and the same promise could survive a season's worth of flyers, banners, and social posts without losing credibility.

把許育璿的視覺語言從「熱血」轉為「可被信任」。地方選戰的視覺往往靠氣氛取勝，但這個案子要解決的是更長期的問題——為許育璿建立一套完整的競選品牌形象識別，讓同一套識別在整個競選週期裡，從旗幟到社群貼文都維持一致且可被信賴的語氣。

02 — Problem

專案背景與痛點梳理Background & Problem

地方選戰的視覺語言常常停留在「熱血、口號式」的表層，但許育璿真正需要被解決的問題是長期的信任感。Local campaign visuals often stay on the surface of "passionate, slogan-driven" cues, but what Xu Yu-Xuan actually needed solved was long-term trust.

許育璿既有的素材多由不同單位、不同時間點製作，色彩、字體、語氣都不一致，選民在不同場合看到的是好幾個版本的「同一個人」。這讓辛苦累積的曝光無法疊加成一致的印象，也讓候選人本身的個性被傳統選戰視覺的套路稀釋掉。

Xu Yu-Xuan's existing materials were produced by different units at different times, with inconsistent colors, typefaces, and tone — voters saw several different versions of "the same person" across different occasions. That meant hard-won exposure couldn't accumulate into a consistent impression, and the candidate's own personality was diluted by conventional campaign-visual conventions.

這次的任務，是把許育璿真實的氣質與訴求，收斂成一套完整的品牌形象識別——可以重複使用、在任何媒材上都站得住的視覺系統。

The task was to distill Xu Yu-Xuan's genuine character and message into one complete brand identity — a reusable visual system that holds up across any medium.

03 — Strategy

設計策略與視覺規劃Design Strategy & Visual Planning

語氣的視覺轉化Translating Tone Into Visuals

把「熱血」這個既定印象拆解成更精確的詞彙——誠懇、穩定、在地——再用色彩與字體去對應許育璿本人的氣質，而不是直接套用選戰視覺的慣性配色。

We broke the default impression of "passionate" down into more precise words — sincere, steady, rooted in the community — then matched color and typeface to Xu Yu-Xuan's own character, instead of defaulting to the usual campaign-visual palette.

品牌識別與媒材的執行策略Brand Identity & Media Execution Strategy

建立一套完整的品牌識別系統與簡潔的版面骨架，從旗幟、看板到社群貼文都套用同一組比例與字級邏輯，讓不同尺寸、不同媒材輸出時，選民都能在第一眼認出是同一個人、同一個承諾。

We built a complete brand identity system with a clean layout skeleton, applying the same proportion and type-size logic from flags and billboards to social posts — so regardless of size or medium, voters recognize the same person and the same promise at first glance.

04 — Design Details

設計深化／從識別到競選現場的應用Design Details — From Identity to Applied Campaign Trail

∞品牌識別與標準色系統Brand Identity & Standard Color System

用一組沉穩而非過度高彩度的標準色，搭配清晰易讀的字體，為許育璿建立起可被信任的第一印象品牌識別。

A steady, non-oversaturated standard color palette paired with a clear, legible typeface gives Xu Yu-Xuan a trustworthy first-impression identity.

∞競選宣傳物設計Campaign Collateral Design

旗幟、看板與傳單沿用同一套版面比例，讓不同尺寸的物料在街頭並列時，仍能維持一致的視覺節奏。

Flags, billboards, and flyers all follow the same layout proportions, so materials of different sizes keep a consistent visual rhythm when standing side by side on the street.

∞社群與數位識別延伸Social & Digital Identity Extension

把品牌識別系統延伸到社群貼文模板，讓許育璿日常的政見說明與行程曝光，也能維持與實體物料一致的語氣與閱讀節奏。

The brand identity system extends into social post templates, so Xu Yu-Xuan's day-to-day policy explainers and schedule updates keep the same tone and reading rhythm as the physical materials.

05 — Conclusion

從熱血口號到可被信任的品牌識別From Passionate Slogans to a Trustworthy Brand Identity

這次為許育璿建立的競選品牌形象識別，把原本零散、隨場合變化的素材，收斂成一套可重複使用的識別語言。選民在不同場合看到的不再是好幾個版本的候選人，而是一個語氣穩定、持續累積信任感的形象。

The campaign brand identity we built for Xu Yu-Xuan pulled what had been scattered, occasion-by-occasion materials into one reusable identity language. Voters no longer see several different versions of the candidate across occasions — they see one steady-toned image that keeps building trust.

重點回顧
Key Points

  • 品牌識別重塑：把許育璿的氣質轉化為穩定、可被信任的標準色與字體系統。
  • Brand identity rebuilt: Xu Yu-Xuan's character translated into a steady, trustworthy standard color and typeface system.
  • 競選物料系統化：建立統一版面比例，讓旗幟、看板、傳單在街頭維持一致節奏。
  • Campaign collateral systemized: a unified layout proportion keeps flags, billboards, and flyers in consistent rhythm on the street.
  • 數位識別延伸：把品牌識別語言延伸至社群貼文模板，串連實體與線上的曝光體驗。
  • Digital identity extension: the brand identity language extended into social post templates, linking physical and online exposure into one experience.
