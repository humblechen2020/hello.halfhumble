築夢造山募資計畫Build Dreams · Mountain Fundraising

TEducation, Social Design PTeaching Project DHanpo Chen Y2020 類別教育 · 社會設計 專案性質教學輔導專案 設計陳翰博（輔導） 年份2020 Link嘖嘖 · 狗狗山流浪動物築夢造山募資計畫 ↗

Mentored students from Taichung Girls' Senior High School in creating the visual identity for a crowdfunding campaign supporting the Dog Mountain Animal-Friendly Association. Responsible for brand strategy, visual identity, key visual design, and merchandise development — demonstrating how design can translate social values into meaningful communication and make things happen.

指導臺中女中學生為狗狗山動物友善協會打造募資計畫的品牌視覺，負責品牌策略、識別發想、主視覺及義賣商品設計。透過真實專案引導學生從設計思考出發，將品牌理念轉化為具說服力的視覺溝通，體會設計不只是創造美感，更是解決問題、凝聚共識與推動社會行動的工具。

02 — Problem

真實任務才能教出真實的設計力Real Briefs Build Real Design Thinking

狗狗山動物友善協會需要能打動陌生人捐款的募資視覺；而台中女中的學生需要一個真實的「為誰設計」。Gougoushan Animal Friendly Association needed fundraising visuals compelling enough to move strangers to donate. Students needed a real "design for someone" brief.

協會長期收容、救援與媒合流浪動物，擁有鮮明的使命，卻缺乏能吸引大眾視線的視覺語言。一般的公益設計往往流於情感勒索，或沿用既有的愛心圖騰，讓整體訴求模糊。這次計畫希望找到另一條路：不靠憐憫，靠認同。

The association has a clear mission — sheltering, rescuing, and rehoming stray animals — but lacked a visual language that could draw a wider audience. Typical charity design tends toward emotional manipulation or recycled heart-shaped icons. This project set out to find another route: not pity, but identification.

學生的設計訓練常常少了「對象」——作業有明確的答案，卻沒有真正的讀者。這次計畫把兩個需求接在一起：讓學生設計一個真實的募資案，用設計換到真實的資源。

Student design training often lacks a real audience — assignments have correct answers but no actual readers. This project connected both needs: students design a real fundraising campaign, and the design earns real resources.

03 — Brand System

狗狗山四大特色Four Pillars of Dog Mountain

從品牌策略工作坊出發，學生將協會四大核心理念轉化為動態視覺模組，讓抽象的價值主張有了可以被看見的形狀。

Starting from brand strategy workshops, students translated the association's four core values into animated visual modules — giving abstract propositions a visible form.

01 · 真心經營，人性規畫
02 · 珍愛生命，妥善安置
03 · 珍視環境，促進生態
04 · 珍重教育，邁向未來
04 — Design Details

主視覺與義賣品設計Key Visual & Charity Sale Items

∞募資主視覺設計Fundraising Key Visual

主視覺必須同時吸引從未聽說過協會的陌生人，以及已認同動物友善理念的潛在捐款者。學生發展出一套以「山」為核心隱喻的視覺系統，呼應協會名稱中的「山」，以及動物在山間自在生活的意象。

The key visual needed to reach both strangers and existing supporters. Students developed a visual system centred on the mountain as a core metaphor — echoing the association's name and the image of animals living freely in the hills.

∞義賣品設計 — 布袋Charity Sale Items — Tote Bags

義賣品是募資計畫的實體觸點，也是讓人願意掏出錢包的理由。學生設計了一套布袋，讓購買者在日常生活中帶著協會的故事繼續走——不是紀念品，而是可以繼續說故事的日常物件。

Charity sale items are the physical touchpoints of a fundraising campaign. Students designed a set of tote bags that let buyers carry the association's story into daily life: not souvenirs, but everyday objects that keep telling the story.

∞義賣品設計 — 周邊商品Charity Sale Items — Accessories

刺繡貼紙與杯帶以協會主視覺為核心元素延伸設計，讓每一件物品都能成為協會理念的小小傳遞者。

The embroidered sticker and cup band both extend from the association's core visual identity, turning each item into a small ambassador for the cause.

05 — Conclusion

設計不只是作品，而是讓事情發生的工具Design Is Not Just a Portfolio Piece — It Makes Things Happen

這個計畫讓台中女中的學生完成了一套真實可用的募資視覺系統，也讓狗狗山動物友善協會得到了一次具體的募款行動。學生在過程中學到的不只是技術——而是「設計為什麼存在」這個更根本的問題的答案。

This project helped Taichung Girls' High School students complete a real, deployable fundraising visual system, and gave Gougoushan Animal Friendly Association a concrete fundraising action. What students learned wasn't just technique — it was the answer to a more fundamental question: why does design exist.

重點回顧
Key Points

  • 真實任務的教學力：學生面對真實的委託對象與募資目標，讓設計決策有了真正的重量。
  • The power of real briefs: students worked against a real client and fundraising goal, giving their design decisions genuine weight.
  • 品牌策略先行：從訪談與田野出發，讓視覺語言有根，而不是空想出來的風格。
  • Strategy before style: starting from interviews and fieldwork, the visual language grew from real roots rather than aesthetic guesswork.
  • 義賣品作為設計的終點：主視覺延伸到可以被人帶走的物件，讓設計從螢幕走進日常。
  • Charity items as a design endpoint: the key visual extended into objects people could take away, moving design from screen to daily life.
