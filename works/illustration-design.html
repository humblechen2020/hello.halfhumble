插畫設計Illustration Design

TIllustration, Competition, Packaging PLab / Self-Initiated DHanpo Chen Y進行中 類別插畫設計 專案性質個人創作 / 競賽入圍 設計陳翰博 年份進行中

A personal illustration collection spanning a 2021 competition shortlist, a self-initiated City Cafe cup concept, seasonal greetings, and a pandemic-era collaboration poster — each one a different reason to pick up the pen.

橫跨 2021 年競賽入圍、City Cafe 牛年包裝自主提案、節慶插畫系列，以及疫情時期的公益合作海報——每一張都是拿起筆的不同理由。

01 — 薛丁格黑貓的神遊之旅

2021 科學與幻想插畫藝術節 · 入圍獎2021 Science & Fantasy Illustration Festival · Shortlisted

一道關於量子不確定性的命題，變成一張關於數位時代迷失的密集插畫。A prompt about quantum uncertainty, turned into a dense illustration about getting lost in the digital age.

「尋找薛丁格的貓」主題插畫大賽以量子力學的不確定性為命題，邀請插畫家回應「既存在又不存在」的狀態。《薛丁格黑貓的神遊之旅》以一隻黑貓穿梭於現實與網路世界的雙重空間為主軸，把薛丁格的量子命題轉譯成一幅關於現代人在資訊洪流裡同時在場又缺席的密集敘事圖。

The "Looking for Schrödinger's Cat" competition invited illustrators to respond to the quantum state of existing and not-existing simultaneously. The Wandering Journey of Schrödinger's Black Cat follows a black cat crossing between the real world and digital space — translating Schrödinger's paradox into a dense visual narrative about modern people simultaneously present and absent in the flood of information.

02 — City Cafe 牛年特別包裝

扭轉青春 · 牛年限定咖啡杯Twist of Youth · Year of the Ox Limited-Edition Cup

把生肖意象從傳統吉祥符文重新解構，畫成一套可以印在紙杯弧面上的角色插畫。Deconstructing the zodiac ox from traditional lucky symbolism into a character illustration fit to wrap around a curved paper cup.

以 City Cafe「扭轉青春」主題為前提的自主提案。牛年限定杯的設計挑戰在於杯身是展開的扇形——圖案在平面上的比例與排布，要對應到立體成杯後的視覺效果。以「扭轉」為核心意象，將牛、7-ELEVEN 店面、傳統符文等元素重新混搭，以高彩度、高密度的插畫風格呈現。

A self-initiated concept built around City Cafe's "Twist of Youth" campaign theme. The challenge: the cup body unfolds into a fan shape — proportions in the flat artwork must map to the visual effect once wrapped around a three-dimensional cup. Taking "twist" as the core motif, the ox, 7-ELEVEN storefronts, and traditional lucky symbols are remixed into a high-saturation, high-density illustration.

03 — 節慶插畫系列

聖誕節 · 跨年 · 新年快樂Christmas · New Year's Eve · Happy New Year

用最大聲的配色，說最簡單的那句「快樂」。The loudest possible color palette, for the simplest possible message: joy.

節慶插畫系列以極度飽和的色彩與密集的圖形元素為共同語言——聖誕節的迷幻聖誕樹、跨年倒數的誇張表情群、牛年的生肖角色——每一張都是用插畫語言在慶祝一個理由。

The seasonal illustration series shares extreme color saturation and dense graphic elements as a common language — a psychedelic Christmas tree, exaggerated countdown faces for New Year's Eve, a Year of the Ox zodiac character. Each one is a reason to celebrate, rendered in illustration.

04 — STAY LOVE NOT ALONE

果皮米刻山 GoGo Shan × 疫情期間公益插畫海報GoGo Shan × Pandemic-Era Charity Illustration Poster

疫情隔開了你和我，也讓心靈無處可躲。在最困難的時刻，有我們的愛與陪伴。The pandemic kept us apart and left nowhere to hide. In the hardest moments, love and companionship remain.

配合狗狗山友善動物公益組織的「STAY LOVE NOT ALONE」主題，以一幅插畫海報回應疫情期間人與動物之間的陪伴關係。九個小房子裡，每一對人與毛小孩都有各自的生活節奏，卻共同組成一個「即便距離，愛不孤單」的視覺宣言。

In collaboration with GOGO SHAN friendly animal welfare organization, this poster responds to companionship between people and animals during the pandemic. In nine small houses, each person-and-pet pair has their own rhythm of life — yet together they form one visual declaration: love is not alone, even in distance.

05 — Conclusion

每一張插畫，都是一個不同理由拿起筆Every Illustration, a Different Reason to Pick Up the Pen

這批插畫作品橫跨競賽、自主提案、節慶創作與公益合作，每一張的起點都不一樣——有的是被量子力學命題觸發的長圖，有的是把傳統生肖畫進一個弧面的工藝挑戰，有的是疫情裡一個需要說出口的「陪伴」。插畫是一種可以跟任何主題合奏的語言，只要有值得說的事。

This collection spans competition entries, self-initiated packaging concepts, seasonal work, and charity collaborations — each starting from a different place. Some were triggered by a quantum mechanics brief, some by the craft challenge of wrapping a design around a curved surface, some by a need to say "you're not alone" in the middle of a pandemic. Illustration is a language that can harmonize with any subject, as long as there's something worth saying.

重點回顧
Key Points

  • 競賽入圍：《薛丁格黑貓的神遊之旅》入圍 2021 科學與幻想插畫藝術節，並參與實體聯展展出。
  • Competition shortlist: The Wandering Journey of Schrödinger's Black Cat was shortlisted for the 2021 Science & Fantasy Illustration Festival and exhibited in a physical group show.
  • 包裝提案：City Cafe 牛年限定杯是自主提案，從杯身的弧面展開幾何出發，把生肖意象轉化成高密度插畫。
  • Packaging concept: The City Cafe Year of Ox cup was a self-initiated project, building the illustration from the geometry of a cup's unfolded fan shape.
  • 系列語言：節慶插畫以極飽和色彩與密集圖形為共同語法，在不同節日的語境下各自發展視覺個性。
  • A series language: The seasonal illustrations share extreme saturation and graphic density as a grammar, each developing its own visual personality within a different holiday context.
  • 公益合作：STAY LOVE NOT ALONE 海報回應疫情時期的陪伴主題，以插畫說出一個需要被說出的情感。
  • Charity collaboration: The STAY LOVE NOT ALONE poster responds to pandemic-era companionship — using illustration to say something that needed to be said.
