Case L05 / 24

攝影作品Photography

TPhotography PLab DHanpo Chen YOngoing 類別攝影 專案性質其他設計類別 設計攝影:陳翰博 年份進行中

The camera is another kind of layout practice — paying attention to light, to composition, to deciding what's worth keeping. This is an ongoing personal collection, outside the brief of any client work.

鏡頭是另一種版面練習——看光、看構圖、看什麼值得被留下。這是一份持續累積中的個人攝影創作，沒有客戶與簡報，只有設計判斷之外，另一種觀看世界的方式。

02 — Problem

創作背景與觀看練習的起點Background & the Starting Point of a Looking Practice

設計工作多半從一個既定的問題出發；攝影則相反，是先看見、再回頭問為什麼值得被留下。Design work usually starts from a defined problem. Photography works in reverse — see first, then ask afterward why it's worth keeping.

在客戶導向的設計專案裡，每一個決定幾乎都有明確的脈絡與目的。攝影成為一個對照組——沒有簡報、沒有客戶，只有當下的光線、構圖與直覺判斷。這種不被任務驅動的觀看方式，反而成為設計判斷之外的一種補充練習。

In client-driven design projects, almost every decision has a clear context and purpose. Photography became a counterpart to that — no brief, no client, just the light, composition, and instinct of the moment. This task-free way of looking turned into a complementary practice alongside design judgment.

這個系列收錄的作品，沒有單一主題，而是持續累積的觀看片段，記錄下不同場景裡值得停留的瞬間。

This collection has no single theme — it's an ongoing accumulation of fragments of looking, recording moments worth pausing on across different scenes.

03 — Strategy

觀看方式與收錄邏輯A Way of Looking & a Logic for Collecting

從設計眼光延伸的攝影直覺Photographic Instinct Extending From a Design Eye

版面、留白與比例的設計訓練，自然延伸進按下快門前的判斷——構圖是否成立、光線是否值得等待，都用同一套設計直覺去檢視。

Design training in layout, negative space, and proportion naturally carries into the judgment made before the shutter clicks — whether a composition holds, whether the light is worth waiting for, all examined through the same design instinct.

作品收錄與整理的方式How the Collection Is Curated

這個系列不追求單一敘事，而是依拍攝場景與題材鬆散分組，持續累積、持續篩選，讓作品集本身也呈現出觀看方式隨時間變化的痕跡。

This series doesn't chase a single narrative — it's loosely grouped by scene and subject, continuously accumulating and being edited, so the collection itself shows traces of how the way of looking has shifted over time.

04 — Works

影像作品選集Selected Works

五個系列，五種觀看。每個場景都是一次短片。

Five series. Five ways of looking. Each scene is a short film.

Scene 01 / 05 · Concept

Embers of the Rose映帆 · 餘燼後的盛開

Between love and fire We burn, then we shatter Like flames, eventually expire But the rose's own beauty will not disappear because of this. On the contrary, after the trial by fire, the bloom after the embers will be even more radiant.

在愛與火之間 我們燃燒，隨後破碎 如同火焰，終將熄滅 但玫瑰自身的光彩，不會因此消盡 經過焰火的試煉，餘燼後的盛開將會更加豔麗

Scene 02 / 05 · Night

這就是人生A Tribute to WOOZI's "Ruby"

以歌曲《Ruby》的氛圍為起點，在城市夜色與霓虹散景裡找尋一顆「美麗的寶石」——色調由我親自調光，讓夜晚的溫度與歌詞裡的迷惘相互呼應。

Taking the mood of "Ruby" as a starting point, searching for a "beautiful jewel" amid city night and neon bokeh — color-graded by hand to echo the song's warmth and quiet disorientation.

看不見 我現在該何去何從 耀眼的燈光拉近了我的雙眼 血紅的炙熱 她引領我踏入 而我在你身上看到了自己 是妳讓我成為了一顆美麗的寶石 ✨

Out of sight Where am I running to now Blinded by shining lights Bloody red flamin' up Pulling me close I see myself in ya Baby girl you're making me a beautiful jewel ✨

Graphic Design: @zhong_type0.0 · Photography: @henpo__chen @akunichi · Model: @cozh_w · Colorist: @henpo__chen

Scene 03 / 05 · Ceremony

Kalala迦納納部落豐年祭

Kalala（迦納納部落）豐年祭，是阿美族人於一年收穫之際舉行的重要慶典。透過祭儀、歌舞與圍圈共舞，向自然與祖靈表達感謝，也在共同的儀式中凝聚部落情感與文化記憶。這不只是慶典，更是一場關於土地、生命與族群連結的延續。
