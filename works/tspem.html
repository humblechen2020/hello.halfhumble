T.S.P.E.M — 年會主視覺與手冊設計T.S.P.E.M — Annual Conference Key Visual & Publication Design

TKey Visual, Publication Design PLong-term Client Project DHanpo Chen Y2020–2025 類別主視覺設計 專案性質長期委託案 設計視覺設計:陳翰博 年份2020–2025

Since 2020, I've designed the annual key visual for T.S.P.E.M. (Taiwan Society of Pediatric Emergency Medicine) — a new illustrated theme every year, from pandemic-era challenges to child-safety campaigns to the academic conference itself, eventually growing into a dual-cover publication system. Six years in, the brand keeps changing its costume without ever losing its face.

從2020年開始，我每年為台灣兒童急診醫學會（T.S.P.E.M.）設計年會主視覺——主題從疫情挑戰、兒少安全到學術年會本身，逐年改變，後期更發展出雙封面的手冊系統。六年下來，這套識別不斷換裝，但始終不換臉。

02 — Problem

年度委託最大的難題：主題每年都不同，識別不能跑掉The Recurring Problem — A New Theme Every Year, One Identity That Can't Drift

從疫情後的兒童急診挑戰到兒少安全網，六年來每一場視覺的題目南轅北轍——如果每次都從零開始發想，學會自己的海報反而會稀釋掉自己的識別。From post-pandemic emergency-care challenges to child-safety campaigns, six years of themes have pulled in very different directions — start from zero every time, and the society's own posters end up diluting its own identity.

台灣兒童急診醫學會每年舉辦年會暨學術演講會，主視覺需求幾乎都是「重新設計」而不是「沿用舊版」——2020年談的是凝聚與守護，2021年因應疫情轉向急診挑戰，同一年還有一場兒少保護專題研討會需要獨立視覺。每個案子單獨看都成立，放在一起卻很難一眼認出是同一個學會。

T.S.P.E.M. holds an annual conference, and nearly every year called for a fresh visual rather than a refresh of the last one — 2020 spoke of unity and protection, 2021 pivoted to post-pandemic emergency-care challenges, and that same year also needed a standalone visual for a child-protection symposium. Each piece worked on its own, but lined up together, they barely read as the same organization.

委託關係持續了下來，問題也跟著浮現：要怎麼讓「每年都不一樣」變成優點而不是風險，同時讓六年後的海報，第一眼還是被認出是同一個學會。

The relationship kept going, and so did the question: how do you turn "different every year" into a strength instead of a risk, so a poster six years later is still recognizable at a glance.

03 — Strategy

策略：鎖住三個錨點，其餘每年自由換裝Strategy — Three Fixed Anchors, A New Costume Every Year

三個不變的錨點Three Anchors That Never Move

學會圓形徽章固定出現在同一個角落、年份用最大級數的數字放在主標題色塊裡、議程資訊永遠收在底部同一條色帶——只要這三個位置守住，其餘的角色、場景、配色都可以每年重新發揮。

The society's circular emblem always sits in the same corner, the year appears at the largest type size inside the title block, and the program details always live in the same color band at the bottom. Hold those three positions steady, and everything else — characters, scenes, color — is free to reinvent each year.

雙封面手冊：一套插畫，兩種身份A Dual-Cover Handbook — One Illustration Cast, Two Identities

2024年起，年會手冊正式分成兩本：「演講講義」給聽講座的人，「會員大會手冊」給參加會員大會的人。兩本共用同一批插畫角色與排版骨架，只用色調區分身份——翻開現場一眼就能拿對本子。

Starting in 2024, the conference handbook split into two: a "Lecture Handout" for session attendees and a "Member Assembly Handbook" for the general meeting. Both share the same cast of illustrated characters and the same layout skeleton, distinguished only by color — so on-site, picking up the right book is a glance, not a guess.

執行策略：把規則交給下一年的自己Execution Strategy — Handing the Rules to Next Year's Self

因為錨點固定、配色邏輯清楚，每年重新提案時不需要從零討論版面，只需要決定當年的角色與場景。這讓六年的合作關係維持得住，學會也不用每年重新教育新的設計師認識自己的識別。

Because the anchors are fixed and the color logic is explicit, each year's proposal starts from the scene and characters, not the layout. That's what kept a six-year client relationship running smoothly — the society never has to re-teach a new designer who it is.

04 — Design Details

設計深化／六年裡的不同應用場合Design Details — Six Years, Many Occasions

∞從兒少安全到會場現場：海報之外的延伸應用Beyond the Poster — Child-Safety Campaigns & On-site Signage

同一套設計邏輯也支援學會的周邊活動：2021年的兒少保護專題研討會需要一張獨立、面向社福與教育單位的海報；2024年會則需要能直接掛進會場走廊的指標型輸出。兩者都不是「主視覺」本身，卻同樣得在第一眼就讓人知道「這是T.S.P.E.M辦的」。

The same design logic also supports the society's adjacent activities: a 2021 child-protection symposium needed a standalone poster aimed at social-welfare and education audiences, while the 2024 conference needed signage that could hang straight in a venue corridor. Neither is the "key visual" itself, yet both still had to read as T.S.P.E.M. at first glance.

∞雙封面手冊：會員大會手冊，一年一個顏色The Member-Assembly Cover, A New Color Every Year

「會員大會手冊」延續同一套插畫角色站位，2024年用暖橘，2025年換成粉色——色彩成為唯一隨年份轉動的變因，讓老會員光看顏色就能分辨「這是今年的」。

The "Member Assembly Handbook" keeps the same cast in the same poses — warm orange in 2024, pink in 2025. Color becomes the one variable that turns over each year, so returning members can tell "this year's edition" by color alone.

∞海報實際上牆：會場視覺現場The Poster, Installed

設計最終要在會場的燈光、動線與人流裡接受檢驗。2025年的海報立牌實際掛進會場大廳，驗證了整套色彩與排版規則在實體空間裡依然站得住。

A design is only finished once it survives the lighting, traffic flow, and crowd of an actual venue. The 2025 standee, installed in the conference lobby, proved the whole color-and-layout system still holds up in physical space.

05 — Conclusion

六年過去，年會主視覺依然認得出來Six Years On, Still Recognizably the Same Conference

從2020年的第一張海報到2025年的雙封面手冊與會場立牌，T.S.P.E.M.的視覺主題年年不同，但徽章位置、年份級數與配色邏輯始終沒有換過。這份長期委託案證明了「每年都不一樣」可以是品牌資產，而不是辨識風險。

From the first poster in 2020 to the dual-cover handbooks and venue standee of 2025, T.S.P.E.M.'s visual themes have changed every year — but the emblem's position, the year's type scale, and the color logic never have. This long-running engagement proves that "different every year" can be a brand asset rather than a recognition risk.

重點回顧
Key Points

  • 長期主視覺系統：六年下來主題年年不同，徽章位置與配色邏輯卻維持一致。
  • A long-running visual system: six different annual themes, with the emblem position and color logic staying consistent throughout.
  • 多場合延伸應用：同一套邏輯支援研討會海報、會場指標與會員手冊等不同物料。
  • Multi-occasion extension: the same logic supports symposium posters, venue signage, and member handbooks alike.
  • 實體場域驗證：設計最終在會場燈光與人流中接受檢驗，證明系統站得住。
  • Proven in physical space: the system held up once tested under real venue lighting and foot traffic.
