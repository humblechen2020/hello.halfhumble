Brookesia — 美妝品牌形象企劃與商品拍攝Brookesia — Beauty Campaign Direction & Product Photography

TBeauty, Campaign PCommercial DHanpo Chen Y2024 類別形象拍攝 專案性質商業合作 年份2024 AD@henpo__chen Photo@akunichi、@henpo__chen、@zac._.tw、@z7jsnry MUA@z7jsnry

Brookesia is a Korean beauty brand. For its spring "garden" campaign — shot in collaboration with influencer @ula.0602 — our team handled the whole visual pipeline end to end: creative concept, shoot planning, lighting, set design, beauty retouching, and post. The goal was a single, coherent world where the products' texture and the talent's persona belong together.

Brookesia 是一個韓國美妝品牌。這次的春日花園主題形象企劃，攜手網紅 @ula.0602 合作，團隊負責從創意發想、拍攝企劃、燈光配置、場景設計，到美妝修圖與後期製作的完整視覺整合——目標是讓產品的質感與人物的氣質，活在同一個世界裡。

02 — Problem

專案背景與課題梳理Background & Problem

美妝形象不能只是把產品拍清楚——它要同時承載品牌調性與聯名網紅的個人氣質，兩者若各說各話，整組視覺就散了。A beauty campaign isn't just about photographing the product cleanly — it has to carry both the brand's tone and the collaborating influencer's personal aura at once. When those two speak in different languages, the whole set of visuals falls apart.

Brookesia 的產品本身質感細緻，色調溫柔；而聯名的網紅 @ula.0602 有一套清新、自然的個人形象。難點在於：一組形象視覺要同時服務「賣產品」與「經營人設」兩件事，光線、場景與調色只要稍微偏掉，就會變成「網美照」或「電商主圖」其中一種，失去品牌想要的平衡。

Brookesia's products have a delicate texture and a gentle palette, while the collaborating influencer @ula.0602 carries a fresh, natural personal image. The challenge: one set of visuals had to serve both "selling the product" and "building the persona" at once. Push the lighting, set, or grading even slightly and it collapses into either an influencer selfie or an e-commerce hero shot — losing the balance the brand wanted.

這次的任務，是把產品、人物與場景收進同一套視覺邏輯裡，讓一次拍攝同時產出能用在電商、社群與品牌形象的素材。

The task was to bring product, talent, and set under a single visual logic — so one shoot would produce material usable for e-commerce, social, and brand image all at once.

03 — Strategy

視覺定調與拍攝企劃Visual Direction & Shoot Planning

先定一個世界Set the World First

從品牌的花卉標誌與溫柔色調出發，先把整組拍攝定調成一座「春日花園」：低彩度的綠、新鮮的花材、柔和的自然光。所有產品、人物與道具都收進這個世界，視覺才有一致的根。

Starting from the brand's floral mark and gentle palette, we first set the whole shoot as a "spring garden": low-saturation greens, fresh flowers, soft natural light. Every product, person, and prop lives inside that world — giving the visuals one consistent root.

一次拍攝、多種用途One Shoot, Many Uses

拍攝企劃同時排進三種畫面：人物與產品的形象照、單品特寫、以及可裁切成不同比例的延伸素材。光線與構圖邏輯先定好，現場就能在同一套規則下快速產出電商主圖、社群貼文與品牌形象需要的不同版本。

The shoot plan scheduled three kinds of frames at once: image shots of talent with product, single-product close-ups, and extendable material that crops to different ratios. With lighting and composition logic locked in advance, the set could quickly produce the e-commerce hero shots, social posts, and brand-image versions needed — all under the same rules.

04 — Production Details

製作深化／從場景到後期的實際執行Production Details — From Set to Post

∞場景設計與拍攝企劃Set Design & Shoot Planning

以真草、苔蘚與多種花材搭出立體的花園佈景，呼應品牌花卉標誌。拍攝前先列好分鏡與道具配置，確認每個角度都接得回同一個世界，減少現場臨時決策。

We built a layered garden set from real grass, moss, and an assortment of flowers to echo the brand's floral mark. Before the shoot, a shot list and prop layout ensured every angle connected back to the same world, reducing last-minute decisions on set.

∞燈光配置與美妝修圖Lighting & Beauty Retouching

用柔和的主光保留皮膚與產品本身的細節，避免過曝洗掉妝感與質地。後期的美妝修圖以「乾淨但不假」為原則，統一膚色與產品色調，讓質感真實又一致。

A soft key light preserved the detail of both skin and product, avoiding overexposure that washes out makeup and texture. Beauty retouching followed a "clean but not fake" principle — unifying skin tone and product color so the texture stays both real and consistent.

∞形象照與聯名延伸Image Shots & Collaboration Extension

除了單人與產品畫面，也規劃了雙人形象照，延伸聯名合作的故事性與社群話題，讓同一套視覺語言能在品牌與網紅雙方的通路上都用得上。

Beyond solo and product frames, we planned paired image shots to extend the collaboration's storytelling and social buzz — letting the same visual language work across both the brand's and the influencer's channels.
