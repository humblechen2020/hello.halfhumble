The Poet’s Woven Sunshine｜Fashion Image Design
編太陽的詩人｜服飾形象設計

靈感取自梵谷的〈向日葵〉系列，
自我是那凋零中的夏天——
熱烈奔放與陰鬱頹喪交織，
在光與影的邊界曖昧游移，
性感而感性，柔軟卻堅定。

Inspired by Van Gogh’s Sunflowers,
the self becomes a withering summer—
a fusion of radiant passion and quiet melancholy.
Hovering in the ambiguous space between light and shadow,
it is sensual, sensitive, and softly defiant.

四套主題服飾——《新生》《嫩芽》《躊躇》《母愛》——如同人生旅途中四段微妙的情感篇章，細膩而飽滿。為了讓服裝不只是靜態展示，而能在影像中轉化為有機情境語言，創作團隊在選角、燈光、場景與拍攝氣氛上悉心打磨，致力完成一次情境與服裝之間深刻對話的轉譯。


The four themed outfits—"New Bron," "Sprouting," "Hesitation," and "Maternal Love"—represent four delicate and emotionally rich chapters along the journey of life. Rather than presenting the garments as static displays, the creative team sought to transform them into living, contextual expressions through imagery. 

Every detail—from model selection and lighting to setting and atmosphere—was carefully refined, aiming to realize a profound dialogue between emotion, clothing, and context.

標準字設計融合了編織般的線條質地、文藝復興時期的形式秩序，以及詩人書寫時流動的情感節奏。透過結構與筆勢的精緻統整，文字呈現出如同服飾一般柔韌且富有溫度的美學語言，展現獨特且深具層次的視覺表現。

The logotype design weaves together the textured quality of woven lines, the formal order of the Renaissance, and the fluid emotional rhythm of a poet’s handwriting. Through the integration of structure and stroke, the letterforms embody a visual language that mirrors the garments’ soft resilience and warmth, conveying a unique and layered aesthetic.

《新生》
臥躺在溫暖的土壤.
包裹快樂的.
快樂的皮囊.
無數個.
輕輕捧起.
將你迎向空氣迎向殘酷的謊言.

《New Bron》
Lying in the warmth of the soil, Wrapped in happiness,
A fragile shell of joy.
One by one, countless forms, Gently lifted.
We raise you toward the air,
Toward the cruel weight of lies.

《嫩芽》
迎向光明的前途.
看似弱小的根脈.
擴張生命般的攀爬向上只為了一口陽光下的沐浴.
在每一個空洞中灌溉自己溫暖的空間.

《Sprouting》​​​​​​​
Reaching toward a radiant future.
Seemingly fragile roots
climb and stretch upward like life unfolding,
all for a single moment of sunlight’s embrace.
Within every hollow,
they nourish a space of warmth from within.

《躊躇》
每日都在懊悔下.
耳邊風聲響起.
許多注目的眼睛觀看一場精彩絕倫的演出.
期盼你能夠閃耀奪目期盼你高高的墜下.

《Hesitation》
Each day, lived beneath the weight of regret.
The wind whispers past my ears,
as countless watching eyes
gather for a breathtaking performance.
They long to see you shine—
and long to watch you fall.

《母愛》
枯萎的雙手輕輕捧起稚嫩的生命.
最後一章精彩的落幕.

《Maternal Love》

Withered hands gently cradle a tender life.
The final chapter draws to a brilliant close.
