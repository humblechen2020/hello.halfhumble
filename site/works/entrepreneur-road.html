文化部文化資產局 — 創業路主視覺設計Bureau of Cultural Heritage — Entrepreneur Road Key Visual Design

TVisual, Event PCase Study DHanpo Chen Y2022 類別主視覺設計 專案性質實例專案 設計視覺設計:陳翰博 年份2022

Commissioned by the Bureau of Cultural Heritage, this key visual project breaks a multi-stage event into a single visual rhythm that carries across posters, social media, and video. Rather than designing one static image, the brief asked for a beat that could repeat and evolve as the event itself unfolded in phases.

把活動的階段感拆成可以延伸到海報、社群、影像的同一條視覺節拍。文化部文化資產局主辦的這場活動分多個階段推進，主視覺需要的不是一張好看的圖，而是一套能隨著階段演進、又維持一致節奏的視覺系統。

02 — Problem

專案背景與痛點梳理Background & Problem

文化機關主辦的活動常分階段推進，但主視覺若只做成一張靜態海報，很難承載整場活動的時間感與多階段敘事。Events hosted by cultural institutions often unfold in stages, but a key visual built as a single static poster struggles to carry the sense of time and multi-stage narrative of the whole event.

這場由文化部文化資產局主辦的活動，內容涵蓋多個推進階段，從前期宣傳、活動現場到後續影像紀錄，過去類似的活動視覺往往各階段各自設計，導致整體曝光看起來破碎，觀眾很難感受到活動本身的進程與張力。

This event, hosted by the Bureau of Cultural Heritage, spans multiple stages — from pre-event promotion, to the event itself, to the follow-up documentation. In similar past events, visuals for each stage were often designed independently, leaving the overall exposure feeling fragmented and making it hard for audiences to sense the event's progression and momentum.

這次的任務，是找到一條可以貫穿所有階段的視覺節拍，讓海報、社群貼文與影像素材即使在不同時間點釋出，也能被感知為同一場活動的不同章節。

The task was to find a visual beat that could run through every stage, so that posters, social posts, and video materials released at different times would still read as different chapters of the same event.

03 — Strategy

設計策略與視覺節奏規劃Design Strategy & Visual Rhythm Planning

階段感的視覺轉化Translating a Sense of Stages Into Visuals

把活動的時間軸拆解成幾個可被識別的「節拍」，用色彩深淺與圖形密度的漸進變化，讓觀眾即使只看到單一階段的素材，也能感受到整場活動的推進感。

We broke the event's timeline down into a few recognizable "beats," using gradual shifts in color depth and graphic density — so even if viewers see only one stage's materials, they can still sense the event's overall momentum.

主視覺與影像的執行策略Execution Strategy for the Key Visual & Video

建立一套可被套用在海報、社群與影像素材上的視覺規則，讓不同單位、不同時間點產出的內容，都能依循同一條節拍，不必每次重新設計，也維持整體活動敘事的連貫性。

We established a set of visual rules applicable across posters, social media, and video materials, so content produced by different teams at different times could follow the same beat — no redesign needed each time, while keeping the overall event narrative coherent.

04 — Design Details

設計深化／從節拍到多媒材的實際應用Design Details — From Beat to Applied Multi-media Reality

∞活動主視覺與階段系統Event Key Visual & Stage System

主視覺以漸進的圖形密度對應活動的不同階段，讓單張海報也能暗示整場活動的時間推進。

The key visual uses progressive graphic density to correspond to the event's different stages, so even a single poster can hint at the event's overall progression through time.

計劃理念上，「創業路的十二堂基本功」開設不同面向的創業課程，打好基礎並建立可實際運用的技巧與知識，全程教授密集且最新的社群行銷工具，內容涵蓋媒體佈局實務、商品攝影實務、自媒體經營實務、網路運營實務課程，以及店面服務與陳列實務課程五大面向 —— 這五大面向也成為五張階段海報各自的設計主題。

The program's underlying concept: The Entrepreneurial Journey — 12 Essential Skills You Need to Succeed opens up entrepreneurship courses across different fronts, building a solid foundation along with practical, usable skills, while teaching intensive, up-to-date social marketing tools throughout. The curriculum spans five areas — media placement practice, product photography practice, self-media management practice, online operations practice, and storefront service & display practice — and these five areas became the individual design themes behind the five stage posters.

∞社群與數位版型延伸Social & Digital Template Extensions

把視覺節拍延伸到社群貼文版型，讓日常的活動預告與花絮，也能維持與主視覺一致的節奏感。

The visual beat extends into social post templates, so everyday teasers and behind-the-scenes content also keep the same rhythm as the key visual.

∞影像紀錄與現場應用Video Documentation & On-site Applications

現場指標、舞台視覺與影像紀錄沿用同一套節拍邏輯，讓活動結束後的影像素材，也能延續主視覺的敘事節奏。

On-site signage, stage visuals, and video documentation all follow the same beat logic, so post-event footage continues the key visual's narrative rhythm.

05 — Conclusion

讓多階段活動有同一條節拍Giving a Multi-stage Event a Single Beat

這次為文化部文化資產局設計的活動主視覺，把原本容易破碎的多階段活動內容，收斂成一條可以延伸到海報、社群、影像的視覺節拍。即使在不同時間點釋出，觀眾也能感受到這是同一場活動持續推進的章節。

The key visual we designed for the Bureau of Cultural Heritage brought content that easily fragments across multiple stages together into one visual beat that carries across posters, social media, and video. Even released at different times, audiences can sense these are ongoing chapters of the same event.

重點回顧
Key Points

  • 活動視覺節拍化：用殘影圖形把十二堂課程收斂成一條可被識別的視覺節拍。
  • Event visuals turned into a beat: a motion-blur motif unifies twelve courses into one recognizable visual rhythm.
  • 跨物料延伸：同一套系統延伸到海報、社群版型、現場看板與報名 QR code。
  • Cross-material extension: the same system extends to posters, social templates, on-site signage, and registration QR codes.
  • 現場與影像紀錄：主視覺在實際場域中維持一致辨識度，留下完整的活動影像紀錄。
  • On-site & documentation: the key visual stayed consistently recognizable on-site, leaving a complete visual record of the event.
