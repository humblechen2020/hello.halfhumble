2021台灣設計展在嘉義Taiwan Design EXPO 2021, Chiayi

TKey Visual, Proposal PSelf-Initiated DHanpo Chen ADHanpo Chen GDHanpo Chen, Shao-Hua Sun ILLHanpo Chen, Shao-Hua Sun, Chen-Lin Liang Y2021 類別主視覺設計 專案性質自主專案 設計視覺設計:陳翰博 美術指導陳翰博 平面設計陳翰博、孫紹華 插畫陳翰博、孫紹華、梁宸霖 年份2021

Condensing an entire city's character into one expandable key visual — and a cast of district characters that carry it through every corner of the venue. This self-initiated proposal for the Chiayi edition of Taiwan Design EXPO 2021 builds a key visual system alongside a set of illustrated characters representing each exhibition zone.

把城市性格濃縮成一套可展開的主視覺與展區角色插畫。這是台灣設計展嘉義場「嘉義百趴」主視覺設計提案，並延伸展區角色插畫設計。

02 — Problem

專案背景與痛點梳理Background & Problem

城市型設計展的主視覺提案，最難的是把一座城市的性格濃縮成一個圖像，又要展開成多個展區的角色內容。The hardest part of a key-visual proposal for a city-wide design expo is condensing an entire city's character into one image, then expanding it into character content for multiple exhibition zones.

嘉義百趴作為城市行銷與設計展雙重身分的活動，主視覺不能只是好看的圖案——它需要承載「這是嘉義」的地方性，同時要有足夠的延展性，讓不同展區能各自發展出獨特但統一的角色插畫。

As an event that doubles as both city marketing and design exhibition, the key visual couldn't just be a pretty graphic — it needed to carry Chiayi's local identity while staying flexible enough for each zone to develop its own distinct yet unified character illustrations.

這次的任務，是設計一套可以從主視覺一路展開到展區角色插畫的視覺系統，讓整場設計展的敘事在不同空間裡都能被串連起來。

The task was to design a visual system that expands from the key visual all the way to the exhibition-zone character illustrations, linking the design expo's narrative across every space.

03 — Strategy

設計策略與系統規劃Design Strategy & System Planning

城市性格的角色化Turning City Character Into Characters

把嘉義在地的生活意象拆解成「人、文、特、值」四大主題，再展開成25種型態＝「嘉義百怪」，讓每個展區都有自己的代言形象，又共用同一套插畫風格。

We broke down Chiayi's local life imagery into four themes — people, culture, specialty, and value — then expanded them into 25 character types collectively called "100 Creatures of Chiayi," giving each zone its own mascot while sharing one illustration style.

主視覺到展區內容的延伸策略Extension Strategy — From Key Visual to Zone Content

先確立主視覺的色彩與構圖核心——以交趾陶工藝的主要配色「人情味」茶褐為主、古黃／綠玉／海碧／胭脂紅／古藍為輔——再以同一套插畫語言發展出多個展區角色，讓整場設計展從入口主視覺到展區內部都維持一致又有層次的視覺敘事。

We first locked in the key visual's color and composition core — anchored by a jiaozhi-ceramic-inspired palette of warm brown as the primary tone, with ochre, jade, sea-blue, carmine, and indigo as supporting hues — then used the same illustration language to develop multiple zone characters, keeping a consistent, layered visual narrative from the entrance key visual all the way into each exhibition zone.

04 — Design Details

設計深化／從主視覺到展區角色的實際應用Design Details — From Key Visual to Zone Characters

∞主視覺設計與構圖Key Visual Design & Composition

主視覺以嘉義的城市意象為核心，建立一套色彩與構圖規則，作為後續所有應用延伸的視覺基準。

The key visual is built around Chiayi's city imagery, establishing a set of color and composition rules that serve as the visual baseline for every application that follows.

∞主視覺商標槍稿與淘汰版本Key Visual Drafts & Rejected Concepts

在定案前，我們發展了大量商標槍稿，嘗試徽章、字體堆疊、圖像化符號等不同方向，逐輪淘汰後才收斂出最終的主視覺商標。

Before finalizing the design, we explored a large number of logo drafts — badges, stacked typography, iconographic symbols, and more — narrowing them down round by round until we arrived at the final key visual.

05 — Character Roster

嘉義百怪・四大主題角色全陣容The Complete "100 Creatures of Chiayi" Cast — All Four Themes

在正式交付的設計檔案中，我們把人、文、特、值四大主題的角色陣容整理成完整名冊，統一以深色畫布呈現，方便後續印刷與展場輸出時直接套用。

In the final design files, we organized the full character roster for all four themes — People, Culture, Specialty, and Value — onto a unified dark canvas, ready to apply directly to print and venue output.

06 — Outdoor & Venue Applications

展場與戶外大型應用情境Venue & Outdoor-Scale Application Scenarios

從文宣品的桌面構圖，到看板、公車站、旗幟、立牌等戶外大型輸出，我們逐一模擬主視覺在不同尺度與場景下的實際效果，確保整套視覺在任何載體上都站得住。

From tabletop print layouts to large-scale billboards, bus stops, banners, and standees, we simulated the key visual at every scale and setting to make sure the system holds up on any medium.

07 — Collateral, Digital & Apparel

識別配件、數位介面與服飾包袋應用Collateral, Digital Interfaces & Apparel Applications

主視覺與角色插畫也延伸到識別證、徽章、口罩、官網介面、馬克杯，以及服飾與包袋等日常周邊，讓品牌語言能持續出現在觀眾的生活情境裡。

The key visual and character illustrations also extend to lanyards, badges, masks, the official website interface, mugs, apparel, and bags — keeping the brand language present in everyday moments long after the expo.

Character illustrations on apparel and bags use a four-color rotation, shown here as animated files cycling through each colorway of the same character print.

08 — Conclusion

把城市性格展開成一場完整的視覺敘事Expanding City Character Into a Complete Visual Narrative

這套提案把嘉義的城市性格濃縮成一個主視覺核心，再展開成多個展區角色與周邊應用，讓觀眾從踏入展場的第一刻，到走完每個展區，都能感受到同一個城市故事在不同空間裡延續。

This proposal condenses Chiayi's city character into one key-visual core, then expands it into multiple zone characters and applications — so visitors feel the same city story continue across every space, from the moment they step into the venue to the last zone they walk through.

重點回顧
Key Points

  • 城市性格濃縮：把嘉義的地方意象提煉成一個好辨識、可延展的主視覺核心。
  • Condensing city character: distilled Chiayi's local imagery into a recognizable, extensible key-visual core.
  • 展區角色系統：以統一插畫語言發展多個展區角色，兼顧整體一致性與個別辨識度。
  • Zone character system: developed multiple zone characters in a unified illustration style, balancing overall consistency with individual distinctiveness.
  • 視覺敘事延伸：讓主視覺到展場周邊應用維持同一套敘事邏輯，串連整場設計展體驗。
  • Visual narrative extension: kept one narrative logic running from the key visual to every venue application, linking the entire expo experience.
