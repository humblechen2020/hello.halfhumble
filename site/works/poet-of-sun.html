編太陽的詩人 — 服飾形象設計The Poet's Woven Sunshine — Fashion Image Design

靈感取自梵谷的〈向日葵〉系列，
自我是那凋零中的夏天——
熱烈奔放與陰鬱頹喪交織，
在光與影的邊界曖昧游移，
性感而感性，柔軟卻堅定。

Inspired by Van Gogh's Sunflowers,
the self becomes a withering summer—
a fusion of radiant passion and quiet melancholy.
Hovering in the ambiguous space between light and shadow,
it is sensual, sensitive, and softly defiant.

02 — Concept

凋零中的夏天A Withering Summer

熱烈奔放與陰鬱頹喪交織——性感而感性，柔軟卻堅定。四段情感章節，四套人生情境的服裝語言。A fusion of radiant passion and quiet melancholy — sensual and sensitive, softly yet defiantly. Four emotional chapters, four fashion languages for four states of being.

這個作品不從「展示服裝」出發，而是從文學意象出發：梵谷向日葵裡那種同時盛開與凋萎的張力，成為整個視覺系統的情感底色。四套服飾對應四個情感狀態，每一套都有屬於自己的場景、光線與人物詮釋方式。

This project doesn't start from "displaying clothes" but from a literary image: the tension in Van Gogh's Sunflowers of blooming and withering at the same time becomes the emotional underpinning of the entire visual system. Each of the four outfits corresponds to an emotional state, with its own scene, light, and interpretive approach.

設計工作涵蓋三個維度：標準字設計（讓文字本身成為詩的載體）、服飾設計稿（從彩稿到製衣的過程）、情境攝影（室內打光與戶外場景的雙軌拍攝策略）。三者交織，讓這套作品不只是時尚棚拍，更接近一個有完整語境的形象系統。

The design work spans three dimensions: logotype design (making letterforms themselves carry the poem), fashion illustration (from colored sketch to finished garment), and contextual photography (parallel indoor and outdoor shooting strategies). Woven together, the result is less a fashion shoot and more a complete image system with its own world.

03 — Strategy

三個核心，一套形象語言Three Pillars, One Image Language

服飾理念：向日葵的生命弧度Fashion Philosophy: The Arc of the Sunflower

四套服飾各自承載一個情緒狀態——新生的突破、嫩芽的萌發、母愛的包覆、躊躇的游移。剪裁語言以大輪廓、層疊結構與手工質感為主，讓衣物本身能在影像中「說話」，不需要造型強介入。

Each of the four pieces carries an emotional state — NewBorn's breakthrough, Sprouting's emergence, Maternal Love's enveloping quality, Hesitation's wavering. The cutting language favors large silhouettes, layered structures, and handcrafted texture, letting the garment "speak" in the image without heavy styling intervention.

標準字設計：中西書寫的鑲嵌Logotype Design: Interlocking East and West

中文標準字以篆書筆意重構，大幅提升筆觸的張力與辨識性；英文則以義大利體手寫書法呈現，兩套文字在視覺上形成交疊與共生。中文字的金色底調與英文字的橘紅色線條相互穿插，最終形成一個無法拆開的整體。

The Chinese logotype is rebuilt with seal-script brushwork energy, dramatically amplifying stroke tension and legibility; the English takes the form of italic calligraphic script. The two writing systems overlap and coexist visually — the gold tone of the Chinese and the terracotta-orange line of the English interlace into an inseparable whole.

情境轉意：室內打光 × 戶外場景Contextual Transformation: Studio Light × Outdoor Scene

每套服飾同時拍攝室內棚燈版本與戶外情境版本，形成一組對照語言。室內打光強化服裝的結構細節與材質張力，戶外場景則帶入敘事性，讓每套服飾能從「被展示的衣服」變成「正在發生一件事的人」。

Each outfit was photographed in both a studio-lit version and an outdoor contextual version, forming a pair of readings. Studio light heightens the garment's structural details and material tension; outdoor scenes introduce narrative, transforming each outfit from "displayed clothing" into "a person in the middle of something."

04 — Design Details

四個章節，四套形象語言Four Chapters, Four Image Languages

嫩芽 · Sprouting

土壤以下，根鬚最先感知春天。米色與橘黃的堆疊，詮釋破土前夕那種幽微的躍動——不是盛放，是悄悄積蓄能量的沉靜。

Below the soil, the roots feel spring first. Layered beige and amber interpret the quiet stirring just before germination — not yet in bloom, but gathering force in stillness.

新生 · NewBorn

深夜的沙灘，孤絕而遼闊。突破的瞬間不總是光明的——它更接近一種從黑暗中撐開的力量，潮水退去，留下一個全新的輪廓。

A night beach, isolated and vast. The moment of breakthrough is not always lit — it is closer to a force that pries itself open from darkness, the tide retreating to leave a new silhouette behind.

母愛 · Maternal Love

大蓬裙的柔軟輪廓回應的是一種包覆的姿態——不是軟弱，是強大。母愛的形狀是張開的，是把所有東西都攬進來的那種重量。

The full skirt's soft silhouette answers the gesture of enveloping — not weakness, but strength. Maternal love is shaped open, carrying the weight of drawing everything in.

躊躇 · Hesitation

蘆葦叢中，身體語言說的是猶豫。每一個游移的姿勢都是在光與影的邊界做的選擇——留下，或者離去。黑白影像捕捉那個無法下定決心的瞬間。

In a reed field, the body speaks hesitation. Every wavering gesture is a choice made at the border of light and shadow — to stay, or to leave. The monochrome image captures the moment of unresolved decision.

∞四套設計稿總覽All Four Fashion Illustrations

∞拍攝構圖草圖Shooting Composition Sketches

在實際拍攝前，每個場景都有事先設定的構圖邏輯——角色位置、光線方向、身體語言指示——讓攝影師與模特兒在現場有清晰的共同語言，而不是臨場即興。

Before each shoot, every scene had a preset compositional logic — character placement, light direction, body language cues — giving photographer and model a shared visual language on set rather than improvising in the moment.

05 — Conclusion

服飾、影像、文字——三者缺一不可Garment, Image, Text — None Can Stand Alone

這個作品最終達成的，是讓服裝、攝影與字體三個系統相互依存：拿掉任何一個，其他兩個都會失去部分意義。標準字讓影像有了文學性；服飾讓拍攝不只是定格，而是敘事；攝影則讓設計稿從紙面走進了一個真實存在的情境。

What this project ultimately achieves is three systems — garment, photography, and letterform — made mutually dependent: remove any one and the others lose part of their meaning. The logotype gives the images literary weight; the garments give the photography narrative rather than mere stillness; the photography moves the design sketches off the page and into a context that actually exists.

重點回顧
Key Points

  • 文學意象為核心：以梵谷向日葵的情緒張力作為整個視覺系統的情感底色，而非從服裝展示出發。
  • Literary image as core: Van Gogh's Sunflowers provides the emotional underpinning of the entire visual system rather than conventional fashion display logic.
  • 標準字作為詩的載體：中英雙語書法系統交疊共生，讓文字本身成為作品的一個獨立視覺元素。
  • Logotype as poetic vessel: a bilingual calligraphic system of overlapping Chinese and English, making the letterform an independent visual element of the work.
  • 雙軌攝影策略：室內棚燈強化材質張力，戶外場景帶入敘事性，兩者形成對照語言。
  • Parallel photography strategy: studio light amplifies material tension; outdoor scenes introduce narrative — the two form a comparative visual language.
  • 三系統互相依存：服飾、影像、字體三者缺一不可，互相賦予對方意義。
  • Three mutually dependent systems: garment, image, and letterform — none can stand alone; each gives meaning to the others.
