設計現場 3.0 — 線上設計展主視覺與企劃設計Design Scene 3.0 — Online Design Exhibition Key Visual & Program Design

TKey Visual, Curation PInternship Project DHanpo Chen Y2022 類別主視覺設計 專案性質實習專案 設計視覺設計:陳翰博 年份2022

Design Scene 3.0 is an online design exhibition's key-visual and program design project — taking the experience of browsing design work onto the screen, and building a curatorial logic and visual system that gives an online exhibition the same sense of pacing and narrative as a physical show.

把設計現場從實體展覽搬上線上。這是一次以策展思維出發的線上設計展主視覺與企劃設計實習專案，嘗試讓設計作品在沒有實體動線的情況下，依然能透過頁面結構與視覺節奏，被觀眾依序「經歷」。

02 — Problem

專案背景與痛點梳理Background & Problem

線上展覽最容易出現的問題，是把實體展場的內容直接搬上網頁，卻失去了動線與節奏感，變成一份單純的作品清單。The most common pitfall for online exhibitions is moving physical-gallery content straight onto a webpage and losing the sense of flow and pacing — ending up as a plain list of works.

這次實習的任務，是替一個線上設計展規劃主視覺與企劃內容。多數線上展覽容易陷入兩個極端：要不是把作品平鋪直敘地列在頁面上，失去策展邏輯；要不是過度依賴互動特效，反而讓內容本身被淹沒。

This internship task was to plan the key visual and program content for an online design exhibition. Most online exhibitions fall into one of two extremes: laying works out flatly on the page with no curatorial logic, or over-relying on interactive effects that end up burying the content itself.

任務是先確認線上展覽該有的敘事節奏，再用主視覺與頁面企劃，把這個節奏落實到使用者實際瀏覽的路徑上。

The task was to first define the narrative pacing an online exhibition should have, then use the key visual and page planning to translate that pacing into the user's actual browsing path.

03 — Strategy

設計策略與企劃規劃Design Strategy & Program Planning

主視覺的線上轉譯Translating the Key Visual Online

把策展主題轉化成一套可在網頁、社群、宣傳素材上重複使用的關鍵視覺，確保使用者在任何入口接觸到展覽時，都能在第一眼辨認出這是同一個線上設計展。

We translated the curatorial theme into a key visual reusable across the website, social media, and promotional materials, ensuring users recognize the same online exhibition at a glance no matter which entry point they come from.

主視覺的核心概念來自一本「寶石圖鑑」：把每一件正在轉化中的設計作品想像成一顆寶石，探索它生長的環境與變因，揀選獨特閃耀的創作者，鑄造出一個光彩無限延伸的線上世界。

The key visual's core concept is built around a "gem catalog": each design work in the process of transformation is imagined as a gem, exploring the environment and variables behind its growth, selecting the creators who shine uniquely, and forging an online world of endless brilliance.

頁面企劃與瀏覽節奏的執行策略Execution Strategy for Page Planning & Browsing Pace

把展覽內容拆分成幾個邏輯段落，依照「策展主題介紹 — 作品分類 — 設計師訪談」的順序規劃頁面結構，讓使用者在沒有實體動線引導的情況下，依然能依照企劃好的節奏依序瀏覽。

We broke the exhibition content into logical sections, structuring the page in the order of "curatorial introduction — work categories — designer interviews," so users could still browse in the intended sequence without a physical path to guide them.

視覺風格的決策來自一輪關鍵詞研究：從「設計現場」原有三方共構（學師、學生、產業）與多面向整合的精神出發，比對當年的流行語彙——元宇宙、珍珠、溫暖且有力量——並參考 Z 世代與 Y 世代受眾偏好的簡約、復古、文青、台式調性，最終收斂出寶石主題的視覺語言。

The visual style was decided through a round of keyword research: starting from Design Scene's existing three-way structure (educators, students, industry) and its spirit of multi-faceted integration, cross-referencing that year's trending vocabulary — metaverse, pearl, warm yet powerful — and the minimalist, retro, indie, and Taiwanese-style preferences of Gen Z and Gen Y audiences, the process converged on the gem-themed visual language.

04 — Design Details

設計深化／從概念到線上展的實際應用Design Details — From Concept to Applied Online Exhibition

∞線上展主視覺設計Online Exhibition Key Visual Design

以策展主題為核心，發展一套可在首頁、子頁與宣傳社群上重複使用的關鍵視覺，建立展覽的整體識別。

Built around the curatorial theme, this key visual is reusable across the homepage, subpages, and promotional social media, establishing the exhibition's overall identity.

八款寶石圖形分別對應產品設計、工藝設計、包裝設計、空間設計、時尚設計、視覺傳達設計、數位多媒體設計與社會設計八個分類，讓抽象的策展主題轉化成一套可被快速辨識的圖像系統。

The eight gem motifs correspond to eight categories — product, craft, packaging, spatial, fashion, visual communication, digital multimedia, and social design — turning an abstract curatorial theme into a visual system that can be recognized at a glance.

∞線上展企劃與頁面結構設計Online Exhibition Program & Page Structure Design

規劃展覽的頁面架構與內容段落，讓使用者在瀏覽時能感受到策展邏輯，而不只是看到一份作品清單。

We planned the exhibition's page structure and content sections so users sense the curatorial logic while browsing, rather than seeing just a list of works.

∞宣傳素材與社群延伸設計Promotional Materials & Social Extension Design

把主視覺延伸到社群貼文、宣傳 banner 等數位物料，讓展覽資訊能在不同平台上維持一致的識別。

The key visual extends into social posts, promotional banners, and other digital materials, keeping the exhibition's information consistent across different platforms.

05 — Conclusion

把策展的節奏，搬進沒有牆面的線上空間Bringing Curatorial Pacing Into a Wall-less Online Space

這次實習專案，把原本仰賴實體動線的策展邏輯，轉譯成一套線上設計展也能依循的主視覺與企劃結構。即使沒有可以走過的展場，使用者依然能透過頁面節奏，感受到策展試圖傳達的敘事與重點。

This internship project translated a curatorial logic that once relied on a physical path into a key visual and program structure that an online design exhibition can follow as well. Even without a gallery to walk through, users can still sense the exhibition's intended narrative and key points through the page's pacing.

重點回顧
Key Points

  • 主視覺系統設計：以寶石光圈為核心意象，發展出可延伸至八大分類的視覺語言。
  • Key visual system: a radiant gem motif developed into a visual language that extends across eight content categories.
  • 線上展頁面規劃：把實體策展邏輯轉譯成手機與桌機都能順暢瀏覽的頁面結構。
  • Online exhibition planning: translating physical curatorial logic into a page structure that reads well on both mobile and desktop.
  • 社群與宣傳延伸：讓主視覺延伸到社群貼文、限時動態等數位物料，維持跨平台一致識別。
  • Social & promotional extension: the key visual extends into social posts and stories, keeping identity consistent across platforms.
