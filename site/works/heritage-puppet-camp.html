文化部文化資產局 — 皮影戲＋布袋戲夏令營主視覺設計Bureau of Cultural Heritage — Shadow + Glove Puppet Summer Camp Key Visual

TKey Visual, Event PClient Project DHanpo Chen Y2023 類別主視覺設計 專案性質實例專案 設計陳翰博 年份2023

Shadow puppetry and glove puppetry move at completely different rhythms. The brief was to fold both traditions into one key visual for a parent-child summer camp — instantly recognizable to kids, and trustworthy to the parents bringing them.

皮影戲與布袋戲是兩種完全不同節奏的偶戲語言。這次的任務是把它們收進同一套主視覺，讓親子活動的視覺第一眼就能被孩子認出、被家長信任，同時不犧失兩種傳統偶戲各自的識別特徵。

02 — Problem

兩種傳統偶戲，各自的視覺語言Two Puppet Traditions, Two Visual Languages

皮影戲的剪影感與布袋戲的木偶造型，硬湊在一起容易變成兩個各說各話的圖案，而不是一套主視覺。Shadow puppetry's silhouette quality and glove puppetry's carved-figure forms — force them together carelessly and you get two graphics talking past each other, not one key visual.

皮影戲的剪影感與布袋戲的木偶造型，在色彩、線條與材質質感上有著截然不同的傳統脈絡。文化機關主辦的親子夏令營，需要的是一套能同時尊重兩種偶戲傳統、又能讓孩子在第一眼就感到好奇與親近的視覺，而不是把兩種文化符號簡單拼貼在一起。

Shadow puppetry and glove puppetry come from utterly different traditions of color, line, and material texture. A cultural agency's parent-child summer camp needs a visual that respects both traditions while sparking curiosity and approachability for kids at first glance — not a simple collage of two cultural symbols.

這次的任務，是找出一套共同的視覺語法，讓兩種偶戲角色能並存在同一張畫面，又能合奏成一個邀請孩子參加的故事。

The task was to find a shared visual grammar that lets both puppet traditions coexist in one frame, and harmonize into one story that invites kids to join in.

03 — Strategy

設計策略與視覺規劃Design Strategy & Visual Planning

兩種偶戲的共同語法A Shared Grammar for Two Puppet Arts

從皮影戲的剪影輪廓與布袋戲的色塊配置裡，找出共用的線條粗細與配色邏輯，讓兩種偶戲角色能並存在同一張畫面而不互相干擾。

Drawing from shadow puppetry's silhouette outlines and glove puppetry's color-block arrangements, we found a shared line weight and color logic that lets characters from both traditions coexist in one frame without clashing.

親子活動的視覺溝通策略Visual Communication for a Family Event

主視覺以孩子的視角為優先，角色造型簡化但保留偶戲的核心特徵，讓孩子能一眼認出角色屬性，家長也能從色彩與排版判斷出這是值得信任的文化教育活動。

The key visual prioritizes a child's point of view — character forms are simplified yet keep each puppet art's core traits, so kids instantly read what a character is, while parents can tell from color and layout that this is a trustworthy cultural-education event.

04 — Design Details

從概念到視覺的實際應用From Concept to Visual Application

∞品牌規範手冊Brand Guidelines

整套品牌語言收整為規範手冊，定義色彩、字型與角色使用規則，確保後續物料延伸的一致性。

The full brand language is consolidated into a guidelines document defining color, typography, and character usage rules — ensuring consistency across all subsequent materials.

∞周邊商品設計Merchandise Design

把主視覺延伸至實體周邊商品，讓孩子在活動結束後仍能帶著掌中世界的記憶繼續生活。

The key visual extends into physical merchandise, letting kids carry the world of puppetry with them long after the camp ends.

∞活動行程表Event Schedule

活動行程表延續同一套色彩與圖形語言，讓資訊呈現也成為品牌體驗的一部分。

The event schedule continues the same color and graphic language, making information design part of the brand experience.

05 — Social Media

社群媒體延伸Social Media Extensions

從貼文到限時動態，識別系統在不同版型尺寸下保持一致的視覺語言，讓夏令營的宣傳觸及更廣泛的親子族群。

From feed posts to stories, the identity system maintains consistent visual language across different format sizes — extending the camp's reach to families across social platforms.

06 — Conclusion

讓兩種傳統偶戲，合奏成一套孩子想參加的視覺Two Puppet Traditions, Harmonized Into a Visual Kids Want to Join

這套為文化部文化資產局設計的夏令營主視覺，把皮影戲與布袋戲兩種截然不同的偶戲語言，收進同一套兼顧文化尊重與親子親近感的視覺系統。對孩子來說，這是認識傳統文化的第一個邀請；對主辦單位來說，這是一套經得起多種物料延伸的完整視覺方案。

This summer-camp key visual for the Bureau of Cultural Heritage brings shadow puppetry and glove puppetry's two distinct visual languages into one system that honors both traditions while staying approachable for families. For kids, it's a first invitation into traditional culture; for the organizer, it's a complete visual solution robust enough to extend across many materials.

重點回顧
Key Points

  • 共同視覺語法：建立統一的線條與配色邏輯，讓皮影戲與布袋戲兩種傳統在同一張主視覺裡並存不衝突。
  • A shared visual grammar: a unified line and color logic lets both puppet traditions coexist within one key visual without clashing.
  • 親子雙向溝通：角色造型同時滿足孩子的好奇心與家長對文化教育活動的信任感，兩個受眾都找得到入口。
  • Dual-audience communication: character design satisfies children's curiosity and parents' trust in a cultural education event — both audiences find their way in.
  • 完整物料延伸：從主視覺到品牌手冊、周邊商品、活動表、社群媒體，識別系統在每個接觸點都維持一致的視覺體驗。
  • End-to-end collateral: from key visual to brand guidelines, merchandise, schedule, and social media — the identity stays consistent across every touchpoint.
