酣睡貓 — 寢具品牌識別設計Han Shui Mao — Bedding Brand Identity Design

TBrand, Retail PCase Study DHanpo Chen/Ann Y2026 類別品牌識別 專案性質實例專案 設計品牌設計:陳翰博/茜 Ann 年份2026

Han Shui Mao is a bedding brand built around the feeling of a good night's sleep. Using a muted, low-saturation palette and a softer typographic voice, the identity turns "night-time calm" into something that can actually be displayed on a shelf — quiet enough to feel restful, distinct enough to be remembered.

用低彩度與柔軟的字體，把寢具的「夜晚感」做成可被陳列的氛圍。酣睡貓希望傳遞的不是寢具的功能規格，而是入睡前那種安定、放鬆的情緒——這份情緒需要被轉譯成一套在貨架上也站得住的視覺語言。

02 — Problem

專案背景與痛點梳理Background & Problem

寢具市場的視覺語言常陷入兩個極端——過度甜美的居家風，或過度冷調的機能感，都離「想睡」的情緒有點遠。Bedding visuals tend to fall into one of two extremes — overly sweet home-decor style, or overly cold functional style — both a little removed from the feeling of "wanting to sleep."

酣睡貓的產品本質是「幫助入睡」，但市場上多數寢具品牌的視覺仍停留在強調材質規格或促銷訊息的陳列邏輯，色彩與字體都偏向喧鬧，反而與品牌想傳遞的安定感互相矛盾。

Han Shui Mao's product is fundamentally about helping people fall asleep, yet most bedding brands' visuals still follow a display logic built around material specs or promotional messaging — loud colors and typefaces that work against the calm the brand wants to convey.

這次的任務，是找到一套低彩度、柔軟卻不失辨識度的視覺語言，讓品牌在貨架與線上通路上，第一眼就能傳遞「夜晚感」這個核心情緒。

The task was to find a low-saturation, soft yet still distinctive visual language, so the brand could convey its core "night-time" feeling at first glance, both on the shelf and online.

03 — Strategy

設計策略與識別規劃Design Strategy & Identity Planning

情緒的色彩轉化Translating Emotion Into Color

把「夜晚感」拆解成低飽和度的灰藍與暖灰調，搭配柔軟的字體曲線，讓識別本身在視覺上就帶有放鬆、即將入睡的節奏感。

We broke "night-time feeling" down into low-saturation blue-grays and warm grays, paired with soft typographic curves — so the identity itself carries a relaxed, drifting-off-to-sleep rhythm.

識別與陳列的執行策略Execution Strategy for Identity & Display

把柔軟、低彩度的識別語言延伸到包裝與陳列道具上，讓品牌在貨架上即使不靠促銷訊息，也能用氛圍本身吸引消費者多停留幾秒，並留下「這個牌子看起來很好睡」的第一印象。

We extended the soft, low-saturation identity language into packaging and display props, so that even without promotional messaging, the brand's atmosphere alone can hold a shopper's attention a few seconds longer and leave the impression "this brand looks like a good night's sleep."

04 — Design Details

設計深化／從識別到陳列的實際應用Design Details — From Identity to Applied Display

∞品牌識別與字體系統Brand Identity & Typography System

標誌字體選用線條較柔軟的字型，搭配寬鬆的字間距，呼應寢具品牌想傳遞的舒緩感，同時維持辨識度。

The wordmark uses a softer-lined typeface with generous letter spacing, echoing the soothing feeling the bedding brand wants to convey while still keeping it recognizable.

∞產品包裝設計Product Packaging Design

包裝以低彩度色塊搭配簡潔版面，讓寢具本身的材質與色彩成為視覺主角，包裝退居成襯托氛圍的背景。

The packaging pairs low-saturation color blocks with a clean layout, letting the bedding's own material and color take center stage while the packaging recedes into a supporting backdrop.

∞陳列道具與通路應用Display Props & Channel Applications

陳列道具延續同一套色彩語言，讓門市與線上通路的拍攝場景，都能維持一致的「夜晚感」氛圍。

Display props carry the same color language through, so both in-store and online shoot scenes maintain a consistent "night-time" atmosphere.

05 — Conclusion

把睡眠的情緒做成可被陳列的氛圍Turning the Feeling of Sleep Into a Displayable Atmosphere

這次為酣睡貓設計的識別與包裝系統，把「想睡」這個抽象情緒轉譯成一套低彩度、柔軟卻清晰的視覺語言。品牌不再需要靠促銷訊息搶眼，氛圍本身就足以讓消費者在貨架前多停留、多想起這個牌子。

The identity and packaging system we designed for Han Shui Mao translates the abstract feeling of "wanting to sleep" into a low-saturation, soft yet clear visual language. The brand no longer needs to stand out through promotional messaging — the atmosphere alone is enough to make shoppers linger a little longer and remember the brand.

重點回顧
Key Points

  • 品牌識別重塑：用低彩度配色與柔軟字體，把「夜晚感」轉化為可被辨識的視覺語言。
  • Reshaping the identity: low-saturation colors and a soft typeface turn "night-time feeling" into a recognizable visual language.
  • 產品包裝設計：讓包裝退居背景，襯托寢具材質與品牌氛圍本身。
  • Product packaging design: packaging recedes into the background, letting the bedding's material and the brand's atmosphere take the lead.
  • 陳列與通路延伸：建立一致的陳列道具語言，串連門市與線上的品牌體驗。
  • Display & channel extension: a consistent display-prop language links the in-store and online brand experience.
