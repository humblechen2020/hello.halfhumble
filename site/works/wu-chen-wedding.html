吳東翰 & 陳嘉薇 婚禮Tung-Han & Chia-Wei Wedding

TBrand, Wedding PCase Study DHanpo Chen YYear TBC 類別品牌識別 專案性質實例專案 設計品牌設計:陳翰博 年份年份待補

A wedding identity only lives for a single day, yet it has to carry an entire narrative — from the invitation in someone's hands to the visuals on the day itself. For Tung-Han and Chia-Wei, the brief was to translate two people's story into one consistent visual language across every touchpoint of the celebration.

婚禮識別只活一天，卻要撐住整場敘事——從喜帖到主視覺一次定調。吳東翰與陳嘉薇的婚禮品牌與視覺識別設計，把兩人的故事轉譯成喜帖、現場物件與主視覺的一致語言。

02 — Problem

專案背景與痛點梳理Background & Problem

婚禮的視覺物件常常各自為政——喜帖、迎賓看板、桌卡分別找不同的人做，最後拼起來卻像三場不一樣的活動。Wedding visuals are often produced in isolation — invitations, welcome signage, and table cards each made by different people, only to feel like three unrelated events once assembled.

兩人對婚禮的想像很明確：希望整場活動從收到喜帖的那一刻開始，就能感覺到是「同一件事」，而不是把市售模板拼接起來的婚禮現場。但要在有限的籌備時間內，把兩人的故事與喜好濃縮成一套視覺語言，並延伸到所有現場物件，需要先有一套清楚的設計邏輯。

The couple's vision was clear: they wanted the whole event to feel like "one continuous thing" from the moment guests received the invitation, not a wedding assembled from off-the-shelf templates. But condensing two people's story and taste into one visual language, and extending it across every on-site object within a limited timeframe, required a clear design logic from the start.

這次的任務，是建立一套從喜帖到主視覺都通用的識別系統，讓婚禮的每一個物件都在說同一個故事。

The task was to build an identity system that works across everything from the invitation to the key visual, so every object at the wedding tells the same story.

03 — Strategy

設計策略與系統規劃Design Strategy & System Planning

標誌與符號構成 · 待補

兩人故事的視覺轉譯Translating the Couple's Story Into Visuals

從兩人共同的小細節出發，提煉出一個可以重複使用的圖形或符號，作為整套識別的核心，讓每一件現場物件都能回扣到同一個故事。

Starting from small shared details between the couple, we distilled a reusable graphic or symbol as the core of the whole identity, so every on-site object ties back to the same story.

從喜帖到現場的延伸策略Extension Strategy — From Invitation to Venue

先定義一套色彩、字級與版式規則，讓喜帖確定下來的視覺語言，能夠不打折地延伸到迎賓看板、桌卡、菜單等現場物件，維持整場婚禮一致的觀感。

We first defined a set of color, type-scale, and layout rules so the visual language established by the invitation could extend without compromise to welcome signage, table cards, menus, and other on-site objects — keeping a consistent look across the whole wedding.

04 — Design Details

設計深化／從喜帖到現場的實際應用Design Details — From Invitation to On-Site Application

As the first touchpoint of the whole identity, the invitation needed its tone settled first — from paper stock to layout — so guests form an expectant first impression of the wedding the moment they receive it.

05 — Conclusion

把兩人的故事化作一場一致的體驗Turning Two People's Story Into One Consistent Experience

這套識別讓吳東翰與陳嘉薇的婚禮從喜帖開始，到現場每一個物件，都維持著同一個視覺敘事。賓客收到喜帖時建立的期待，在婚禮現場被完整地延續下來——婚禮識別不只是裝飾，而是把兩人的故事說了一遍。

This identity kept Tung-Han and Chia-Wei's wedding telling the same visual story, from the invitation through to every object on site. The expectation guests formed upon receiving the invitation carried through fully to the venue — the wedding identity wasn't just decoration, it retold the couple's story.

重點回顧
Key Points

  • 故事轉譯為符號：從兩人共同的細節提煉出核心圖形，作為整套識別的視覺主軸。
  • Translating story into symbol: distilled a core graphic from shared details between the couple, anchoring the entire identity.
  • 喜帖到現場的一致性：從喜帖開始定義的視覺語言，完整延伸到迎賓看板、桌卡與菜單。
  • Consistency from invitation to venue: the visual language defined by the invitation extends fully to welcome signage, table cards, and menus.
  • 整場體驗的敘事感：讓婚禮的每一個物件都在說同一個故事，而不是各自獨立的裝飾。
  • A narrative sense across the whole experience: every object at the wedding tells the same story, rather than standing as isolated decoration.
