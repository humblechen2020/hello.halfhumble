台中市預拌混凝土商業同業公會Taichung Ready-Mix Concrete Association — Brand Identity

TBrand Identity, Association PClient Project DHanpo Chen Y2024 類別品牌識別 專案性質實例專案 設計陳翰博 年份2024

A trade association identity doesn't need to impress — it needs to be instantly recognized by its own members and trusted on sight by everyone outside the industry. For the Taichung Ready-Mix Concrete Association, the goal was a visual language sturdy enough to hold a member-based body together across official documents, on-site signage, and public-facing events.

公會型組織的識別不需要炫技，需要會員一眼認得、外部一眼信任。台中市預拌混凝土商業同業公會的品牌識別設計，目標是建立一套在公文、看板到活動現場都能保持一致的視覺語言。

02 — Problem

公會視覺的兩個難題Two Problems with Association Identity

對內要凝聚一群各自獨立經營的會員廠商，對外又要在公開場合代表整個產業說話。Internally, it must unite independently run member firms; externally, it must speak for the entire industry in public settings.

在接觸這個案子之前，公會的視覺呈現散落在各種場合裡——會員大會的看板、公文信封上的章、活動現場的旗幟，彼此之間沒有共用的語言，也讓外部單位很難在第一眼就辨認出這是同一個組織。

Before this project, the association's visual presence was scattered — signage at member assemblies, stamps on official envelopes, banners at events — with no shared language between them, making it hard for outside parties to recognize at a glance that these all belonged to the same organization.

任務的核心是釐清公會這個角色本身該如何被看見：既要有產業的扎實感，也要保留組織協調、對話的彈性，而不是套用一般企業品牌的做法。

The core task was clarifying how the "association" role itself should be seen — needing the grounded weight of the industry, while keeping the flexibility of an organization built for coordination and dialogue, not simply applying a typical corporate-branding approach.

03 — Strategy

設計策略：讓三個意象合奏成一個標誌Design Strategy: Three Ideas, One Mark

標誌的三層意象Three Layers in the Mark

標誌由三個元素構成——混凝土車造型象徵產業的扎實基礎，握手意象代表公會的合作與信任精神，太陽圓點象徵活力與希望。三者合奏成一個簡潔可辨的完整標誌，同時帶有「中」字的抽象形態呼應台中地域屬性。

The mark is built from three elements — the concrete mixer truck form represents the industry's solid foundation; the embracing hands represent the association's spirit of cooperation and trust; the red dot represents vitality and hope. Together they compose a clean, recognizable mark that also carries an abstract echo of the character "中" (center/Taichung).

對內凝聚、對外溝通的雙軌策略A Dual-Track Strategy: Internal Cohesion & External Communication

識別系統分成兩條使用情境：對內，會員大會、公文與文件採用較正式的版式規則；對外，公開活動與媒體露出保留更靈活的色彩與排版彈性，讓同一套識別同時服務兩種不同的溝通對象。

The identity system is designed around two scenarios: internally, member assemblies and official documents follow more formal layout rules; externally, public events and media keep more flexible color and layout latitude — letting one identity serve two very different audiences at once.

04 — Identity System

標誌系統與色彩規範Logo System & Color Palette

∞標誌橫式與直式Horizontal & Vertical Logo Versions

標誌提供橫式與直式兩種組合版本，橫式適用於橫向版面（信封、看板），直式作為標準使用版本，適用於名片、文件封面等各種場合。

The mark comes in horizontal and vertical configurations — the horizontal version suits landscape contexts (envelopes, signage), while the vertical version is the standard, for business cards, document covers, and most general applications.

∞品牌色彩系統Brand Color System

以藍綠色為主色建立專業與信任感，灰褐色為輔助傳遞穩健與包容，珊瑚紅作為強調色適度點綴活力。三色組成一套兼顧產業扎實感與組織親和力的色彩語言。

Teal blue anchors the palette as the primary color of professionalism and trust; warm taupe supports it as a stable and inclusive neutral; coral red punctuates as an accent of energy. The three together build a color language that balances industrial solidity with organizational warmth.

∞品牌識別規範手冊Brand Identity Guidelines

完整的識別規範手冊定義標誌版本、最小尺寸、標誌構成邏輯、色彩數值與禁止用法，確保後續所有物料延伸都維持一致的品牌體驗。

A complete brand guidelines document defines logo versions, minimum sizes, mark construction logic, color values, and prohibited usages — ensuring all future collateral maintains a consistent brand experience.

05 — Applications

識別系統的全面落地應用Identity System Applied Across Every Touchpoint

∞名片Business Cards

名片以徽章式識別紋樣為底紋，配合標準化的資訊排版，讓理事長及公會代表在任何正式場合遞出名片時都有清晰且一致的第一印象。

Business cards use the badge-pattern motif as a background texture, paired with standardized information layout — so the chair and representatives make a clear, consistent first impression in any formal setting.

∞公文 · 信封Official Documents & Envelopes

公文與信封是公會對外溝通最頻繁的載體。識別系統在公文信紙上建立清晰的表頭規則，信封以大面積留白搭配識別元素，讓每一份往來公文都成為公會形象的延伸。

Official documents and envelopes are the association's most frequent external touchpoints. The system establishes clear letterhead rules for official stationery, and uses generous white space with identity elements on envelopes — so every piece of correspondence extends the association's image.

∞公會規章冊Guild Regulations Booklet

公會規章冊提供深色與淺色兩種版本，封面以大尺寸識別紋樣作為設計主軸，兼顧正式場合的分量感與視覺辨識度。

The guild regulations booklet is offered in both a dark and a light version — the cover uses an oversized identity motif as the design anchor, balancing the formal weight appropriate for its setting with strong visual recognition.

∞全套文具系統Full Stationery System

從公文信紙、資料夾、名片到筆、橡皮擦、記事本，整套文具系統完整展示識別語言在日常辦公物件上的一致應用。

From letterhead and folders to business cards, pens, erasers, and notebooks — the full stationery system demonstrates the identity's consistent application across everyday office items.

∞工地用品 · 制服Field Equipment & Uniforms

識別系統延伸至工地現場的安全帽與制服 Polo 衫，讓公會在產業現場的露出同樣維持一致的視覺形象，而不僅限於辦公室或正式場合。

The identity extends to hard hats and polo shirts for field use — ensuring the association's presence on job sites carries the same consistent image as in the office or formal settings.

∞LED 牆面標誌LED Wall Signage

辦公場所入口的 LED 牆面標誌以發光霧面效果呈現，讓識別在正式場所的入口也能以具份量感的方式出現。

The office entrance LED wall sign uses a glowing matte effect — giving the identity a weighty presence at the threshold of formal settings.

06 — Conclusion

一套讓組織被看見、被信任的識別An Identity That Makes the Organization Seen and Trusted

這套識別系統讓公會第一次有了一致的視覺身分——會員在內部場合看得到歸屬感，外部單位與媒體也能在第一眼就辨認、信任這個組織。從公文信封到安全帽，從室外看板到公會規章冊，識別不再只是一個標誌，而是公會對內凝聚、對外溝通的共同語言。

This identity system gave the association a consistent visual identity for the first time — members feel a sense of belonging in internal settings, while outside parties can recognize and trust the organization at a glance. From official envelopes to hard hats, from exterior signage to the guild regulations booklet, the identity is no longer just a mark — it's a shared language for internal cohesion and external communication.

重點回顧
Key Points

  • 三層意象合奏：混凝土車、握手、太陽圓點三個元素合奏成一個同時傳遞產業、合作與活力的完整標誌。
  • Three ideas, one mark: the concrete mixer, the embracing hands, and the red dot compose a single mark that communicates industry, cooperation, and vitality simultaneously.
  • 雙軌溝通系統：同一套識別服務內部凝聚與外部溝通兩種情境，維持彈性與一致性的平衡。
  • Dual-track system: one identity serves both internal cohesion and external communication, balancing flexibility with consistency.
  • 全面觸點延伸：從公文信封到安全帽、從室外看板到 Polo 衫，識別在每一個公會接觸點都維持一致的視覺秩序。
  • Complete touchpoint coverage: from official envelopes to hard hats, from exterior signage to polo shirts — the identity maintains consistent visual order at every association touchpoint.
