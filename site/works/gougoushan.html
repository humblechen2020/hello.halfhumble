Case F01 / 04

狗狗山 — 浪犬友善視覺識別Gougoushan — Stray Dog Welfare Visual Identity

TBrand, NPO PCase Study DHanpo Chen Y2024 類別品牌識別 專案性質實例專案 設計品牌設計:陳翰博 年份2024

Reframing the lens: from heavy pity to happy companionship. Gougoushan has worked the front line of stray dog rescue for years, and the brief was to break with animal-welfare's usual playbook of sorrowful appeals. We shifted the public's viewpoint from "feeling sorry for them" to "wanting to get closer" — giving the brand a warm, approachable, energetic tone, so every touchpoint sparks the urge to follow along and keep them company, not just a moment of pity.

黑皮狗狗山長期駐守流浪犬救援第一線，多年來致力於打造不關籠、不綁鍊，與大自然共生的「毛孩子淨土」。然而，傳統動保議題往往伴隨著沉重與悲情訴求，容易讓大眾產生心理距離。2024年，為了讓動保理念更平易近人，協會委託進行全新的視覺識別與品牌重塑。 因應狗狗山期待將大眾視角從「沉重的同情」轉化為「快樂的相處」，在設計上刻意跳脫傳統動保團體悲傷、需要被救援的視覺框架，改以「親近、充滿正向能量」為核心調性。標誌設計將「狗」字巧妙融合高聳山脈與蜿蜒河川的幾何曲線，並大量運用象徵年輕生機的青綠色，以及傳遞溫暖熱情的橘色。透過活潑的色彩對比與流暢的自然意象，我們逐步構築出這個跳脫傳統悲情、讓人渴望靠近且充滿生命力的現代動保品牌形象。

02 — Problem

專案背景與痛點梳理Background & Problem

流浪犬議題的視覺長期停留在「需要被同情」的框架裡，但同情很難轉化成長期的關注與行動。Stray-dog visuals have long been stuck in a "needs your pity" frame — but pity rarely converts into long-term attention or action.

過往多數流浪犬識別習慣用悲情的攝影與沉重的色調來喚起注意，短期有效，但容易讓觀者在情緒消耗後選擇迴避。黑皮狗狗山想要的不是更強烈的悲情訴求，而是一套能長期經營、讓人願意持續追蹤的友善關係。

Most stray-dog identities reach for sorrowful photography and heavy tones to grab attention — effective short-term, but it tends to make viewers look away once the emotion wears thin. Gougoushan didn't want a stronger plea for pity, but a friendly relationship people would keep following over the long run.

我們將大眾的觀看視角，從「覺得可憐」轉化為「渴望靠近」，賦予品牌親近、好相處且充滿正向能量的調性。讓每一次的視覺接觸，都能激發人們對於狗有伴，狗友伴的正確思想。

We shifted the public's viewpoint from "feeling sorry for them" to "wanting to get closer," giving the brand a warm, approachable, positive-energy tone — so every visual touchpoint reinforces the idea that a dog can simply be good company.

03 — Strategy

設計策略與IP設計Design Strategy & Character Design

友善IP轉化Approachable Character Adaptation

把主理人的肢體與表情拆解成可重複使用的視覺語彙，用可愛形象取代寫實的悲情攝影，讓品牌第一眼就傳遞「可以靠近」的訊號。

Deconstruct the founder's body language and facial expressions into a reusable visual vocabulary. By replacing realistic, melancholic photography with endearing imagery, the brand instantly sends a signal of approachability at first glance.

從同情到相處的色彩與語氣Color & Tone: From Pity to Companionship

色彩系統選擇溫暖但不過度甜美的色調，文案語氣也從「請幫助牠們」調整為「來認識牠們」，讓整套識別在社群與現場物料上都維持一致的、好相處的語氣。

The color system leans warm without tipping into saccharine, and the copy tone shifted from "please help them" to "come meet them" — keeping a consistent, easygoing voice across social and on-site materials.

04 — Design Details

設計深化／從概念到應用的實際落地Design Details — From Concept to Applied Reality

∞識別系統設計Identity System Design

建立一套可延展的標誌與色彩系統，讓「友善表情」這個核心概念能被穩定地複製到不同尺度的物料上。

We built an extensible logo and color system so the core "friendly expression" concept can be reliably reproduced across materials of every scale.

∞應用延伸Extended Applications

把識別延伸到送養文宣、社群貼文與現場物料，讓每一次曝光都維持同一種輕鬆好親近的調性。

The identity extends into adoption materials, social posts, and on-site collateral, keeping the same relaxed, approachable tone at every touchpoint.

∞視覺規範Visual Guidelines

整理出一份簡單的使用規範，讓志工與夥伴在沒有設計背景的情況下，也能維持識別的一致性。

We put together a simple usage guide so volunteers and partners without a design background can still keep the identity consistent.

05 — Conclusion

讓議題從同情走向重視Elevating the issue from sympathy to respect.

這次為黑皮狗狗山設計的識別，把流浪犬議題從單向的同情訴求，轉化成一種可以被持續追蹤、願意主動靠近的關係。識別不再只是喚起一次性的憐惜，而是成為長期經營關注度的起點。

The identity we designed for Gougoushan turns the stray-dog issue from a one-way plea for pity into a relationship people actively want to follow and get closer to. It no longer just stirs up a fleeting moment of sympathy — it becomes the starting point for sustained attention.

重點回顧
Key Points

  • 視角翻轉：把流浪犬議題的敘事從同情轉為相處，建立更輕鬆好親近的品牌語氣。
  • Reframing the lens: shifted the stray-dog narrative from pity to companionship, building a more relaxed, approachable brand voice.
  • 表情系統設計：用可延展的標誌與色彩系統，取代寫實悲情攝影作為主要視覺語言。
  • Expression system design: an extensible logo and color system replaces realistic, sorrowful photography as the primary visual language.
  • 志工友善的應用規範：整理簡單易用的規範，讓沒有設計背景的志工也能維持識別一致性。
  • Volunteer-friendly guidelines: a simple, easy-to-use guide lets volunteers without a design background keep the identity consistent.
