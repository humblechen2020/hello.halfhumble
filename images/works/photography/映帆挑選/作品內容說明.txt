《Embers of the Rose》

Between love and fire
We burn, then we shatter
Like flames, eventually expire

But the rose’s own beauty will not disappear because of this.
On the contrary, after the trial by fire, the bloom after the embers will be even more radiant.

在愛與火之間
我們燃燒，隨後破碎
如同火焰，終將熄滅

但玫瑰自身的光彩，不會因此消盡
經過焰火的試煉，餘燼後的盛開將會更加豔麗
