深入地方社區，與公平里里長共同策劃互動式宣傳海報。面對社區世代互動逐漸疏離，以及宣傳預算有限的挑戰，設計策略不只是製作一張海報，而是將居民納入創作過程，邀請孩童自由繪製屬於自己的主視覺，讓作品成為活動宣傳的一部分。透過共同創作，提升社區參與感與活動曝光，最終成功吸引大量居民參與，讓活動獲得熱烈迴響。

Collaborated with the neighborhood chief of Gongping Village to design an interactive campaign for a local community event. Rather than relying on a conventional promotional poster, the project responded to two key challenges—limited promotional resources and a growing disconnect between generations—by inviting children to create their own key visuals. Their artwork became the core identity of the campaign, turning participants into contributors.

This participatory design approach not only reduced promotional costs but also fostered community engagement, strengthened intergenerational interaction, and generated widespread participation, contributing to the event's overall success.
