PROCESS

My process

4 stages · 6—12 weeks
01 — Decompose

拆解

Decompose

3 場深訪：你、你的團隊、你的客戶。我會把你以為是「美感」的問題，重新分類為策略、流程、或溝通的問題。

3 in-depth sessions — with you, your team, and your customers. What looks like an aesthetics problem will be reclassified as strategy, process, or communication.

02 — Direction

方向

Direction

2–3 個方向稿，每一個都附上「為什麼是這個方向」的論證。不是選你喜歡的，是選你回得了家的那個。

2–3 direction proposals, each with a clear rationale. Not the one you like most — the one you can live with long term.

03 — Build

製作

Build

單一方向深化，建立完整系統。期間你會看到 2 次中段確認，避免做完才發現走偏。

One direction deepened into a complete system. Two mid-stage check-ins to catch any drift before final delivery.

04 — Hand-off

交付

Hand-off

所有原始檔、品牌守則、應用範本一次到位。半年內後續微調免費；一年後依比例報價。

All source files, brand guidelines, and application templates in one delivery. Minor adjustments within 6 months complimentary; beyond one year, quoted proportionally.
